Hermit Purple Bridger Western – Complete Stand Guide 2026

Hermit Purple is a DEFAULT Non-Humanoid Utility stand in Bridger Western. Its Utility Stand passive means summoning it costs no stamina. The Grapple (E) deals 10 damage and pulls you toward surfaces, or pulls players toward you if aimed at them. Chop (R) deals 10 damage and performs a dropkick if used after landing E on a player. Players searching for Bridger Western Hermit Purple stand should note it is DEFAULT rarity, Non-Humanoid type, and obtainable through standard Arrow Shard or Corpse Part rolls.
