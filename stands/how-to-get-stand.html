How to Get Stands
Complete Acquisition Guide

Master both Arrow Shard fishing and Corpse Part KOTH events. Learn water escape tactics, server selection strategies, and avoid common mistakes that cost you your Stand.

Bridger Western Stand Acquisition Methods

🎣 Arrow Shard Fishing (Safe but Time-Consuming)

Success Rate: 0.5% drop rate per fish

Risk Level: Very Low

Time Investment: High (luck-based)

Best For: Solo players, risk-averse players

Pros: No PvP risk, can be done solo, guaranteed safety
Cons: Extremely luck-based, very time-consuming

💎 Corpse Part KOTH Events (Fast but Competitive)

Success Rate: 15-20% with proper holding time

Risk Level: Very High

Time Investment: Low (if successful)

Best For: Experienced players, clan members

Pros: Higher success rate, faster acquisition
Cons: Intense PvP competition, requires skill/luck

🎣 Arrow Shard Fishing Complete Guide

⚠️ Critical Information

  • ▸ Drop Rate: 0.5% chance per fish caught
  • ▸ Cannot be stored: Arrow Shards cannot be banked or stored
  • ▸ Cannot be dropped: No way to give to other players
  • ▸ Cannot be traded: Personal use only
  • ▸ Lost on death: Death or leaving server destroys Arrow Shard
  • ▸ Usage requirement: Must not have existing Stand to use

Step-by-Step Fishing Guide

1. 🚂 Find Tackle Bait Shop

Navigation Method:

  • • Follow the train tracks from any location
  • • Look for train stops along the route
  • • Tackle Bait shop is at the second train stop
  • • Alternative: Wait for train and ride it
  • • Use horse (press H) to travel faster along tracks

2. 🛒 Purchase Equipment

Required Items:

  • • Talk to the NPC inside the shop
  • • Select "Buy Gear" option
  • • Purchase a fishing rod
  • • Purchase fishing bait
  • • Ensure you have enough Moola for both

3. 🎣 Fishing Process

Fishing Mechanics:

  • • Find any water source (rivers, lakes, ponds)
  • • Equip fishing rod and approach water
  • • Watch for blue indicator near your character
  • • Click left mouse button when blue appears
  • • Repeat until Arrow Shard drops (0.5% chance)

🚨 Arrow Shard Safety Protocol

Immediate Actions Upon Getting Arrow Shard:

  1. Stop fishing immediately
  2. Check you have no existing Stand
  3. Use Arrow Shard right away
  4. Do not travel or engage in combat
  5. Stay in safe area until used

Why This Protocol Matters:

  • • Arrow Shards are extremely rare (0.5%)
  • • Death means losing hours of fishing work
  • • Cannot be recovered once lost
  • • No insurance or backup methods
  • • Must start fishing process over completely

💎 Corpse Part KOTH Events Guide

📊 KOTH Event Mechanics

Spawn Requirements & Timing:

  • • Minimum players: 3 players in server
  • • Check interval: Every 30 seconds
  • • 3-player server: ~1/150 chance per check
  • • 25-player server: ~1/40 chance per check
  • • Announcement: "IT APPEARS ONCE AGAIN"

Visual & Audio Indicators:

  • • Yellow beam: Visible from anywhere on map
  • • Glowing area: Marks exact landing spot
  • • Loud sound: Audio notification to all players
  • • Time limit: Yellow beam lasts ~30 seconds
  • • Server-wide alert: Everyone knows location

Probability Accumulation System

Critical Discovery: Corpse Parts don't instantly give Stands. You must hold them for time to build up success probability.

Initial Pickup

~5% chance

Immediate use has very low success rate

10-15 Seconds

~10-12% chance

Moderate improvement with holding time

15-20+ Seconds

~15-20% chance

Maximum success rate achieved

Strategy Implication: The longer you survive while holding the Corpse Part, the higher your chances of getting a Stand. This is why survival tactics are crucial.

🌊 Water Escape Tactics - "The Water Method"

🏆 Why Water Escape is the Meta Strategy

The "Water Method" is currently the most effective survival strategy for Corpse Part holders. It exploits game mechanics to make you nearly impossible to catch while building up your success probability.

Advantages:

  • • Speed: Move like a speedboat across water
  • • Unpredictability: Hard to track your movement
  • • Distance: Quickly get far from spawn point
  • • Stamina recovery: Can recover while moving
  • • Terrain advantage: Most players avoid water

Success Rate:

  • • Solo players: 40-60% success with water method
  • • vs Land escape: 5-15% success rate
  • • Time gained: 15-20+ seconds easily achievable
  • • Pursuit difficulty: 90% of chasers give up
  • • Consistency: Works in most server situations

Complete Water Method Execution

1. 🏃 Immediate Response

Upon Corpse Part Pickup:

  • • Don't panic: Stay calm and focused
  • • Press H immediately: Get on your horse
  • • Identify nearest water: Rivers, lakes, ponds
  • • Ignore other players: Don't engage in combat
  • • Head straight to water: Shortest route possible

2. 🌊 Water Entry & Movement

Water Navigation:

  • • Enter water on horseback: Don't dismount
  • • Constant movement: Never stop moving
  • • Speedboat style: Fast, erratic patterns
  • • Use full stamina: Maximum speed initially
  • • Change directions: Unpredictable movement

3. ⚡ Stamina Management

Recovery Technique:

  • • When stamina low: Press S key (backwards)
  • • Backward speed: ~20 speed while recovering
  • • Still moving: Maintain distance from pursuers
  • • Recovery time: 3-5 seconds usually enough
  • • Resume forward: Back to full speed movement

🎯 Advanced Water Tactics

Optimal Water Bodies:

  • • Large lakes: Most space for maneuvering
  • • Rivers: Good for linear escape routes
  • • Connected waterways: Multiple escape options
  • • Deep water: Harder for enemies to follow
  • • Avoid shallow areas: Slower movement speed

Psychological Warfare:

  • • Distance discouragement: Most players give up
  • • Time pressure: They know you're building probability
  • • Effort vs reward: Chasing becomes inefficient
  • • Multiple targets: Other Corpse Parts may spawn
  • • Stamina disadvantage: Chasers tire faster

🎯 Server Selection Strategy

🔍 Finding Low-Competition Servers

Step-by-Step Process:

  1. Open server list: Click on server browser tab
  2. Sort by player count: Click "Descend" then "Ascend"
  3. Target 3-5 players: Minimum for spawns, low competition
  4. Check server stability: Avoid servers with fluctuating counts
  5. Join and monitor: Watch for Corpse Part announcements

Pro Tip: Bookmark good servers and return to them regularly.

📊 Server Population Analysis

High Population (15+ players):

  • • Spawn rate: Very high (1/40 chance)
  • • Competition: Extreme (10+ competitors)
  • • Success rate: 5% for solo players
  • • Clan advantage: Massive

Low Population (3-5 players):

  • • Spawn rate: Lower (1/150 chance)
  • • Competition: Minimal (2-4 competitors)
  • • Success rate: 40-60% for solo players
  • • Wait time: Longer between spawns

⚠️ Special Stand Warnings

🦽 Tusk Stand - Critical Warning

Acquisition Requirements:

  • • Specific body part: Must be LEFT ARM Corpse Part
  • • Success rate: 10% chance with left arm
  • • Other parts won't work: Right arm, legs, ribs = no Tusk
  • • Team coordination: Save left arms for Tusk seekers

Severe Negative Effects:

  • • Permanent disability: Cannot walk, only crawl/roll
  • • No weapons: All guns and sabers deleted
  • • Horse dependency: Must rely on horse for movement
  • • Vulnerability: Extremely vulnerable without protection

⚠️ Before Getting Tusk, Consider:

  • • Do you have friends to protect you? Tusk users need constant protection
  • • Do you have a legendary/mythical horse? Essential for mobility
  • • Are you prepared to never walk again? This is permanent
  • • Can you handle extreme vulnerability? You'll be an easy target
  • • Is the aesthetic worth the gameplay sacrifice? Consider carefully

📋 Body Part Distribution Strategy

When farming Corpse Parts with a team, coordinate based on desired Stands:

Left Arm Corpse Parts:

  • • Tusk seekers get priority
  • • 10% chance for Tusk
  • • Most valuable for specific builds

Other Body Parts:

  • • Right arm, legs, ribs
  • • Give to non-Tusk seekers
  • • Still valuable for other Stands

Team Coordination:

  • • Communicate in Discord
  • • Plan distribution before events
  • • Maximize team efficiency

🚫 Common Mistakes & How to Avoid Them

❌ Critical Errors That Cost You Stands

Arrow Shard Fishing Mistakes:

  • • Continuing to fish after getting Arrow Shard - Use immediately!
  • • Traveling with Arrow Shard - Death risk too high
  • • Trying to use with existing Stand - Won't work
  • • Attempting to store or trade - Impossible, will be lost

Corpse Part KOTH Mistakes:

  • • Using Corpse Part immediately - Low success rate
  • • Staying near spawn point - Everyone will find you
  • • Fighting instead of escaping - Wastes time, high risk
  • • Not having escape plan - Panic leads to mistakes
  • • Trying in high-pop servers - Too much competition

✅ Correct Strategies for Success

Arrow Shard Best Practices:

  • • Fish in safe, remote locations - Avoid populated areas
  • • Use Arrow Shard immediately upon getting it - No delays
  • • Check Stand status before fishing - Clear existing Stand first
  • • Have Root ready for rerolls - Plan for unwanted results

Corpse Part Best Practices:

  • • Execute water escape immediately - Don't hesitate
  • • Hold for 15-20 seconds minimum - Build success rate
  • • Use low-population servers - 3-5 players ideal
  • • Have multiple escape routes planned - Adaptability key
  • • Stay calm under pressure - Panic causes mistakes

🔄 Stand Reset with Root

🌿 Root Item Usage

What Root Does:

  • • Removes current Stand: Completely resets your Stand slot
  • • Allows new acquisition: Can get new Stand after using Root
  • • No Stand recovery: Previous Stand is permanently lost
  • • Immediate effect: Works instantly upon use

Strategic Planning: Always plan your target Stand before using Corpse Parts or Arrow Shards to minimize Root usage.

🤝 Clan Root Advantages

Why Clans Have Better Access:

  • • Shared resources: Clan members pool Root items
  • • Farming coordination: Organized Root farming expeditions
  • • Member support: Help each other get desired Stands
  • • Knowledge sharing: Learn Root locations and spawn times

Solo Player Strategy: Focus on getting your desired Stand on first try through proper planning and server selection.

Bridger Western Stand Acquisition - Complete 2026 Guide

Learning how to get Stands in Bridger Western requires mastering both Arrow Shard fishing and Corpse Part KOTH events. Arrow Shard fishing offers a safe but time-consuming method with a 0.5% drop rate per fish, while Corpse Part KOTH events provide faster acquisition through competitive PvP scenarios. The revolutionary water escape method has transformed Corpse Part survival strategies, allowing solo players to achieve 40-60% success rates compared to traditional 5-15% land-based escapes.

Bridger Western Corpse Part mechanics feature a probability accumulation system where holding the Corpse Part for 15-20 seconds increases success rates from 5% to 15-20%. Server selection strategy plays a crucial role, with 3-5 player servers offering optimal balance between spawn rates and competition levels. Special Stands like Tusk require specific left arm Corpse Parts and come with severe gameplay restrictions including permanent disability and weapon restrictions, making them high-risk, high-reward choices for dedicated players.

Advanced Bridger Western Stand acquisition involves understanding body part distribution, water navigation tactics, stamina management, and psychological warfare against pursuers. The Root item system allows Stand resets but requires strategic planning to minimize waste. Whether pursuing S-tier Stands through clan coordination or solo farming through fishing methods, success in Bridger Western Stand acquisition depends on preparation, execution, and understanding the complex mechanics that govern Ridge B Valley's supernatural power system.
