Hierophant Green Bridger Western – Complete Stand Guide 2026

Hierophant Green is a DEFAULT Humanoid stand in Bridger Western. The Tentacle Wire (E) places tripwires wherever thrown. Emerald Splash (R) is a hold-to-charge powerful projectile blast. The 20 Meter Emerald Splash (G) is a zone-control ability — place tentacle wires, go invisible, and auto-blast emeralds at anyone who enters the area. Stand Pilot (V) lets you take aerial control of your stand. Players searching for Bridger Western Hierophant Green stand should note it is DEFAULT rarity and obtainable through standard Arrow Shard or Corpse Part rolls.
