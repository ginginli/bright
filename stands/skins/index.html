Bridger Western Stand Skins – Complete Shiny Variants Guide 2026

Bridger Western Stand skins are cosmetic variants obtained through a 5% chance on any obtainment method that rolls for your Stand. This includes Arrow Shard fishing (0.1% drop rate) and Corpse Part KOTH events. Bridger Western shiny stands do not affect gameplay — they are purely visual. Available skins include The World skins (Greatest High, OVA), Star Platinum skins (HFTF, Intro, Judge, OVA, Strength), D4C skins (Cracked, M4C), TWAU skins (Manga, Neo, STW), Hierophant Green skins (HFTF, Purple, Red, Yellow), Hermit Purple skins (Hermit Blue, Hermit Gold, Holy's Stand), Mandom skins (King Mandom, Mintdom, Purple Mandom, Reverse Mandom), and Magicians Red skins (Purple).
