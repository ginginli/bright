🎯 Obtainment

Star Platinum | The World is obtained by evolving Star Platinum. When using the G (Time Stop Movement) ability on Star Platinum, there is a 1% chance to automatically evolve into this stand.

This stand cannot be obtained through standard Arrow Shard or Corpse Part rolls.

Star Platinum The World Bridger Western – Complete Stand Guide 2026

Star Platinum | The World (SPTW) is a DEFAULT Humanoid stand in Bridger Western and the evolved form of Star Platinum. It is obtained exclusively by using the G (Time Stop Movement) ability on Star Platinum, which has a 1% chance to trigger the evolution. Its moveset includes a Rush (R) uppercut into mid-air barrage, Triple Bearing Shots (T) for ragdoll, a 2-second Time Stop (G) with medium radius, and a Grab Beatdown (V). Players searching for Bridger Western Star Platinum The World should note it cannot be rolled directly and must be evolved from Star Platinum.
