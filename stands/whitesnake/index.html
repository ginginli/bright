Whitesnake Bridger Western – Complete Stand Guide 2026

Whitesnake is a RARE Humanoid stand in Bridger Western. Its barrage deals 1.4 dmg/tick. The signature Disc Grab (R) grabs forward for 20 damage and rips out a disc, disabling the target's stand. Disc Throw (T) deals 13 damage, explodes on surfaces, and inverts controls on direct hit. Acid Spit (G) creates an acid pool dealing 15 + 3/tick damage that puts anyone inside to sleep. Pilot (V) lets you control your stand remotely. Players searching for Bridger Western Whitesnake stand should note it is RARE rarity and obtainable through Arrow Shard fishing or Corpse Part KOTH events.
