Hey Ya Bridger Western – Complete Stand Guide 2026

Hey Ya is a DEFAULT Non-Humanoid Utility stand in Bridger Western. It has no active moves — its power comes entirely from two passives. The Utility Stand passive means no stamina cost to summon. The Encouragement passive guides you along the right path and provides random buffs including dodges, damage buffs, healing, speed buffs, and increased accuracy. Players searching for Bridger Western Hey Ya stand should note it is DEFAULT rarity, Non-Humanoid type, and obtainable through standard Arrow Shard or Corpse Part rolls.
