Silver Chariot Bridger Western – Complete Stand Guide 2026

Silver Chariot is a DEFAULT Humanoid stand in Bridger Western. The Rapier Uptilt (R) slices upward and briefly launches you into the air. Lunge (T) deals ~20 damage with slight homing, and at low HP it transforms into Ricochet Shot — a rapier that bounces off surfaces and homes in after the 3rd ricochet. Whirlwind Slash (G) hits all nearby targets twice. Armor Removal (V) is the signature ability, greatly boosting speed and damage with special visual effects. Players searching for Bridger Western Silver Chariot stand should note it is DEFAULT rarity and obtainable through standard rolls.
