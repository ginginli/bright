⚡ Skills & Abilities

Passive — Poison Bulbs

Barrage has a chance to burst a poison bulb, leaving a poison cloud. R is guaranteed to burst bulbs. Note: your own poison can hurt you.

Moves

  • E: Barrage — chance to burst poison bulbs on hit
  • R: Bulb Smash — heavy hit; guaranteed to burst poison bulbs on collision
  • T: Poison Bulb Shoot — shoot a bulb at the cursor; creates a poison cloud or deals light damage on direct hit
  • G: Poison Bulb Trap — place a bulb on the ground; bursts if walked over; explodes after ~20 seconds
  • V: Stand Pilot — control your stand from a distance; both your body and stand can take damage

🎯 Obtainment

Obtained through standard stand rolls via Arrow Shard fishing or Corpse Part KOTH events.

Any obtainment roll has a 5% chance to grant a Shiny skin.

Purple Haze Bridger Western – Complete Stand Guide 2026

Purple Haze is a DEFAULT Humanoid stand in Bridger Western with a Poison Bulbs passive — barrages have a chance to burst bulbs leaving poison clouds, and R is guaranteed to burst them. Be careful: your own poison can hurt you. Bulb Smash (R) is a heavy hit that always bursts bulbs. Poison Bulb Shoot (T) fires a bulb at the cursor. Poison Bulb Trap (G) places a ground trap that bursts when walked over and explodes after ~20 seconds. Stand Pilot (V) lets you control your stand remotely, though both your body and stand remain vulnerable. Players searching for Bridger Western Purple Haze stand should note it is DEFAULT rarity.
