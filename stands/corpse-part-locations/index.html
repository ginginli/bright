⚡ KOTH Tips

🔊 How to Detect a Spawn

  • Listen for the loud sound effect
  • Look for the yellow beam visible in the sky
  • The beam lasts approximately 30 seconds
  • Requires 3+ players in the server to spawn

🌊 Water Escape Method

  • Grab the Corpse Part immediately
  • Mount your horse (N) and sprint (Shift)
  • Head to water and hold Space to cross
  • Keep moving — the whole server will chase you

⚠️ Important Notes

  • Corpse Parts spawn at random from these 8 locations — no fixed order
  • Holding a Corpse Part gives a 15–20% chance of obtaining a Stand
  • The Left Arm specifically gives a 10% chance for Tusk (causes permanent disability)
  • For full KOTH mechanics, see the Corpse Part Survival Guide
