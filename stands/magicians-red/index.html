Magicians Red Bridger Western – Complete Stand Guide 2026

Magicians Red is a DEFAULT Humanoid stand in Bridger Western. The Flaming Roundhouse Kick (R) hits all targets in a 180° arc, knocking them away and applying burn. Red Bind (T) fires cursor-aimed flame waves that deal heavy burn damage and restrict movement. The signature Crossfire Hurricane Special (G) launches a large fire projectile — pressing G again redirects it to your cursor and splits it into up to 5 projectiles. Stand Piloting (V) provides aerial control. Players searching for Bridger Western Magicians Red stand should note it is DEFAULT rarity and obtainable through standard Arrow Shard or Corpse Part rolls.
