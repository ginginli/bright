📊 Tier Ranking Criteria & Methodology

🎯 Evaluation Framework

Our tier rankings are based on comprehensive analysis across multiple performance categories, weighted for the current 2026 Alpha meta environment.

⚔️ PvP Performance (40%)

  • ▸ Damage output: Raw damage potential
  • ▸ Combo potential: Ability to chain attacks
  • ▸ Mobility options: Movement and positioning
  • ▸ Defensive capabilities: Survival and protection
  • ▸ Range effectiveness: Close vs long-range performance
  • ▸ Skill ceiling: Potential in expert hands

🏞️ PvE Performance (25%)

  • ▸ Farming efficiency: Speed of content clearing
  • ▸ Survivability: Safety in dangerous areas
  • ▸ Utility functions: Non-combat applications
  • ▸ Resource generation: Money/item farming capability
  • ▸ Solo viability: Independent operation
  • ▸ Progression support: Helps character development

🎮 Accessibility & Meta (35%)

  • ▸ Acquisition difficulty: How hard to obtain
  • ▸ Learning curve: Ease of mastery
  • ▸ Meta relevance: Current competitive viability
  • ▸ Versatility: Multiple use cases
  • ▸ Consistency: Reliable performance
  • ▸ Future potential: Likely meta evolution

  4. Practice KOTH survival: Develop water escape and survival skills
  5. Target A-tier Stands: Star Platinum, TWAU as stepping stones
  6. Clan coordination: Work toward S-tier acquisition

  • • Direct S-tier pursuit: Focus on clan coordination
