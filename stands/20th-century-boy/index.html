20th Century Boy Bridger Western – Complete Stand Guide 2026

20th Century Boy is a DEFAULT Non-Humanoid Utility stand in Bridger Western. Its Utility Stand passive means no stamina cost to summon. Absolute Defense (G) crouches you into an impenetrable fortress that reflects all incoming attacks back at attackers. Dynamite Strap (V) pulls loose dynamite and attaches it to your body for 12 dmg per dynamite — requires 6 dynamite to use, and Absolute Defense negates the self-damage. Players searching for Bridger Western 20th Century Boy stand should note it is DEFAULT rarity, Non-Humanoid type, and obtainable through standard Arrow Shard or Corpse Part rolls.
