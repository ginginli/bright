Tubular Bells Bridger Western – Complete Stand Guide 2026

Tubular Bells is a DEFAULT Non-Humanoid Utility stand in Bridger Western. Its Utility Stand passive means no stamina cost to summon. It has three balloon types: Medium Balloons (R) — 2 Dog balloons at 15 dmg that pop on walls and auto-track close range; Light Balloons (T) — 3 Snail balloons at 5 dmg that pass through walls and auto-track far range; Heavy Balloons (G) — 1 Swan balloon at 40 dmg that follows your cursor. The TUBULAR BELLS!!! (V) fires all balloons at once toward the cursor at reduced damage. Players searching for Bridger Western Tubular Bells stand should note it is DEFAULT rarity and obtainable through standard rolls.
