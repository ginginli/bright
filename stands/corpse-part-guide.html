📊 Complete Spawn System Breakdown

Spawn Requirements:

  • • Minimum players: 3 players required in server
  • • Check frequency: Every 30 seconds exactly
  • • Random calculation: Probability based on player count
  • • No cooldown: Can spawn back-to-back if lucky
  • • Server-wide event: Affects all players simultaneously

Probability by Server Population:

  • • 3 players: ~1/150 chance (0.67%)
  • • 5 players: ~1/120 chance (0.83%)
  • • 10 players: ~1/80 chance (1.25%)
  • • 15 players: ~1/60 chance (1.67%)
  • • 25+ players: ~1/40 chance (2.5%)

  • ▸ Universal Alert: No way to miss the announcement

  • ▸ 0-5 seconds: Rush to location phase
  • ▸ 5-15 seconds: Competition and combat phase
  • ▸ 15-30 seconds: Yellow beam disappears
  • ▸ Post-pickup: Survival and probability building
  • ▸ Next check: 30 seconds from last spawn check

⚠️ Drop Risk Factors

What Causes Corpse Part Loss:

  • • Taking damage: Any hit has drop chance
  • • Being knocked down: High drop probability
  • • Limb damage: Body part breaks can cause drops

The drop chance appears to be around 15-25% per hit received, making damage avoidance absolutely critical for success.
