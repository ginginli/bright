1. Buy Fishing Gear

2. Fish at Any Water Source

  • • Equip rod near water
  • • Cast and wait for bite
  • • Complete the QTE to reel in

3. Use When Ready

Arrow Shards are not lost on death, so there is no rush. Use it when you are in a safe location without an existing Stand.

  • • Make sure you have no Stand
  • • Use from inventory
  • • Receive a random Stand

🎲 What Stand Will I Get?

The Arrow Shard grants a random Stand from the obtainable pool. It cannot give:

  • Unobtainable Stands (e.g. Whitesnake — developer/admin only)
  • Stands with specific obtainment methods (e.g. D4C requires 100 kills + 10 KS in Deserts area; Tusk requires Left Arm Corpse Part)

All other Stands in the pool have an equal chance of being granted. See the full list on the Stands Database.

⚠️ Tusk Warning: If you receive Tusk Acts 1-3, you will permanently lose the ability to walk and use weapons. Consider this before using an Arrow Shard if you are not prepared for that playstyle.

❓ Arrow Shard FAQ

Is the Arrow Shard lost when I die?

No. Arrow Shards are not lost upon death or leaving the server. You can safely carry it in your inventory and use it whenever you are ready.

Can I store the Arrow Shard in the bank?

No. Arrow Shards cannot be banked or stored. They also cannot be dropped or traded to other players. They stay in your personal inventory only.

What is the Arrow Shard drop rate?

0.1% per fishing attempt (Legendary rarity). On average, you need approximately 1000 fishing attempts to obtain one. It shares the same drop rate as the Golden Bass.

Can I use an Arrow Shard if I already have a Stand?

No. Arrow Shards cannot be used if you already possess a Stand. You must remove your current Stand first (via Wipe or the Mud Witch's Stand removal service).

Will the Arrow Shard give me any Stand?

It grants a random Stand from the obtainable pool — guaranteed. It cannot give unobtainable Stands (like Whitesnake) or Stands with specific obtainment methods (like D4C or Tusk).

Is Arrow Shard fishing worth it?

It depends on your playstyle. Arrow Shard fishing is the only fully solo, zero-PvP-risk Stand acquisition method. The trade-off is the grind (~2000 casts average). If you want to avoid KOTH competition and clan politics, it is the best option.

Arrow Shard Bridger Western – Complete 2026 Guide

The Arrow Shard Bridger Western is the Legendary fishing drop that guarantees a Stand when used. With a Bridger Western Arrow Shard drop rate of 0.1%, players need approximately 1000 fishing attempts on average to obtain one. Unlike Corpse Part KOTH events, Arrow Shard fishing Bridger Western requires no PvP, no clan, and no coordination — making it the preferred method for solo players. The Bridger Western Arrow Shard has a glowing, smoky aura whilst held and is not lost upon death or leaving the server, so there is no urgency to use it immediately.

How to get Arrow Shard Bridger Western: purchase a fishing rod (150 Moola) and bait (15 Moola) from Tackle Bait | Fishing Goods, then fish at any water source. All water sources share the same drop rates. Bridger Western Arrow Shard restrictions include: cannot be banked or stored, cannot be dropped or traded, and cannot be used if you already possess a Stand. The Bridger Western Arrow Shard Stand result is random from the obtainable pool — it cannot give unobtainable Stands or Stands with specific obtainment methods like D4C or Tusk.

Players often ask is Arrow Shard lost on death Bridger Western — the answer is no, it persists through death and server leaves. Bridger Western Arrow Shard vs Corpse Part: Arrow Shards guarantee a Stand at 100% but require ~1000 fishing attempts, while Corpse Parts offer 15-20% success rate but involve intense PvP competition. For players without a clan, Arrow Shard Bridger Western fishing is the most reliable path to obtaining a Stand. See the full Stand Acquisition Guide and Fishing Guide for more details.
