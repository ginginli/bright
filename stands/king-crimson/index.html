King Crimson Bridger Western – Complete Stand Guide 2026

King Crimson is a RARE time-manipulating Humanoid stand in Bridger Western. The Donut (T) deals high damage and impales targets. Epitaph (G HOLD) is a defensive ability that auto-dodges nearly any attack while held. Time Erasure (V) grants invisibility, iframes, and a speed boost for approximately 5 seconds — and critically, R and T deal increased damage immediately after activating it. Players searching for Bridger Western King Crimson stand should note it is RARE rarity and obtainable through Arrow Shard fishing or Corpse Part KOTH events.
