Mandom Bridger Western – Complete Stand Guide 2026

Mandom is a RARE time-manipulating Non-Humanoid Utility stand in Bridger Western. Its Utility Stand passive means no stamina cost to summon. The sole active ability is Time Rewind (G) — twist back the watch to return you and everyone around you 6 seconds back in time, reverting all health values and states. Notably, Mandom's Time Rewind is one of the few time abilities not cancelled by Gold Experience Requiem's Return To Zero. Players searching for Bridger Western Mandom stand should note it is RARE rarity, Non-Humanoid type, and obtainable through Arrow Shard fishing or Corpse Part KOTH events.
