DEFAULT HUMANOID Special Obtainment

⚡ Skills & Abilities

Passive — Dimension Hop

While wearing a poncho: taking >20 damage in one blow sends you to another dimension briefly, negating the damage. Putting on or removing the poncho does the same. Nearby players are also brought into the dimension with you.

Moves

  • E: Chop Barrage — high damage, slow
  • R: Tracking Chop — stand follows and chops the target
  • T: Clone Spawn — spawns a clone with slightly less max HP that shoots the nearest target; variants: knife, pipe, shotgun, or pistol
  • G: Clone Swap — swap positions with your clone (camera faces the swap direction); works while downed; lowers max HP until respawn; in poncho dimension: teleports behind any player brought in

🎯 Obtainment

D4C has a very specific special obtainment method:

  • Reach 100 kills with a 10 killstreak
  • Be in the Deserts area
  • Get knocked while a corpse part spawns
  • This gives a 10% chance for a heart to spawn

It cannot be obtained through standard Arrow Shard or Corpse Part rolls.

D4C Bridger Western – Complete Stand Guide 2026

D4C is a DEFAULT Humanoid stand in Bridger Western with the Dimension Hop passive — while wearing a poncho, taking >20 damage in one hit sends you to another dimension briefly, negating the damage. Nearby players are dragged in too. Clone Spawn (T) creates a clone that shoots the nearest target in knife, pipe, shotgun, or pistol variants. Clone Swap (G) swaps your position with the clone and even works while downed. D4C has a unique obtainment: reach 100 kills with a 10 killstreak in the Deserts area, get knocked while a corpse part spawns for a 10% heart spawn chance.
