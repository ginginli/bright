Star Platinum Bridger Western – Complete Stand Guide 2026

Star Platinum is a RARE time-manipulating Humanoid stand in Bridger Western. Its barrage deals 2.2 dmg/hit, and the Skull Breaker (R) hits for 25 damage with an execution variant that decapitates at low HP. Star Finger (T) deals 12 damage and ragdolls targets. The G move (Time Stop Movement) allows movement during timestop and has a 1% chance to evolve Star Platinum into Star Platinum | The World. The Beatdown (V) grabs and pummels for 11 | 2.4/punch | 14 damage. Players searching for Bridger Western Star Platinum stand should note it is RARE rarity and obtainable through Arrow Shard fishing or Corpse Part KOTH events.
