The World Bridger Western – Complete Stand Guide 2026

The World is a RARE time-manipulating Humanoid stand in Bridger Western. Its signature ability is the Long Timestop (~5 seconds) activated with V, making it one of the most powerful stands for PvP. The Checkmate passive causes your stand to throw knife barrages alongside you when using knives. The R move, Shinei, is a long-range autolock punch that can also be used for mobility. Players searching for Bridger Western The World stand should note it is classified as RARE (time-manipulating) and obtainable through Arrow Shard fishing (0.1%) or Corpse Part KOTH events.
