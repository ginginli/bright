⚡ Skills & Abilities

Passive — Thread Weaving

M1 on a surface places a thread attached to your arm. M1 on a person while a thread is attached pulls them toward you.

Moves

  • E: Barrage — 1.5 dmg/tick, punches in front
  • R: Heavy Punch — 25 dmg, dashes forward with thread extending the arm
  • T: String Pull — attach a string to a nearby person and pull them in
  • G: String Patchworks — heal yourself using string
  • V: Rock Swing — 12|12|12|12|20 dmg; pull earth from the ground, swing it, and slam

🎯 Obtainment

Obtained through standard stand rolls via Arrow Shard fishing or Corpse Part KOTH events.

Any obtainment roll has a 5% chance to grant a Shiny skin.

Stone Free Bridger Western – Complete Stand Guide 2026

Stone Free is a DEFAULT Humanoid stand in Bridger Western with a Thread Weaving passive — M1 on surfaces places threads attached to your arm, and M1 on a person while threaded pulls them in. The Heavy Punch (R) deals 25 damage with a forward dash. String Pull (T) pulls nearby targets toward you. String Patchworks (G) heals yourself. The signature Rock Swing (V) pulls earth from the ground and slams for 12|12|12|12|20 damage. Players searching for Bridger Western Stone Free stand should note it is DEFAULT rarity and obtainable through standard Arrow Shard or Corpse Part rolls.
