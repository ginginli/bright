⚡ Skills & Abilities

Passive — True Life Giver

A green bar, 2.5× bigger than Gold Experience's Life Giver bar. Allows 5 moves to be used simultaneously.

Moves

  • E: Barrage (buffed) — QTE activates 7 Page Muda dealing 90 damage
  • R: 4 Variants — Life Giver Punch / Tree / Eagle / Scorpion Shot (45 dmg)
  • T: Self/Other Heal — heals 50% HP; ragdolls the target
  • G: Return To Zero — nullifies incoming attacks, reverts targets to zero, ragdolls for 5 seconds; 60-second cooldown; cancels time abilities except Mandom and Time Erase
  • V: Variant Switcher — cycles between R move variants

🎯 Obtainment

Gold Experience Requiem is classified as UNOBTAINABLE / SPEC and cannot be obtained through standard gameplay rolls. It is a developer/special stand.

Gold Experience Requiem Bridger Western – Complete Stand Guide 2026

Gold Experience Requiem (GER) is an UNOBTAINABLE/SPEC stand in Bridger Western. Its True Life Giver passive is a green bar 2.5× larger than Gold Experience's, allowing 5 simultaneous moves. The 7 Page Muda QTE (E) deals 90 damage. Return To Zero (G) is the signature ability — it nullifies all incoming attacks, reverts targets to zero, and ragdolls for 5 seconds with a 60-second cooldown. Notably, it cancels all time abilities except Mandom and Time Erase. Players searching for Bridger Western Gold Experience Requiem should note this stand is not obtainable through normal gameplay.
