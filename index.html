Bridger Western Wiki & Trello - Complete 2026 Alpha Database & Guides

Bridger Western is a Roblox survival game set in the cursed wilderness of Ridge B Valley, blending western frontier gameplay with JoJo's Bizarre Adventure Stands and Jujutsu Kaisen cursed techniques. This wiki serves as the most comprehensive Bridger Western Trello alternative — covering everything the official Trello doesn't, including detailed Stand acquisition guides, KOTH probability mechanics, water escape tactics, and Discord clan strategies. Unlike most western bridger trello pages, we provide verified gameplay data with specific drop rates (Arrow Shard 0.5%, Corpse Part 15-20% success rate) and step-by-step guides.

The Bridger Western stands list includes all available Stands: The World, D4C, Killer Queen, Star Platinum, TWAU, King Crimson, Star Platinum The World, Tusk Acts 1–3 (which causes permanent disability — you cannot walk or use weapons), Hierophant Green, Silver Chariot, Hermit Purple, Tubular Bells, 20th Century Boy, Gold Experience, Crazy Diamond, Hey Ya, Mandom, Purple Haze, and Whitesnake (unobtainable). Arrow Shards can be obtained through fishing at a 0.5% drop rate and guarantee a Stand when used. The water method — escaping to water on horseback after grabbing a Corpse Part — is the most reliable solo survival tactic, as aquatic terrain makes it difficult for pursuers to follow.

Beyond Stands, Bridger Western features a brutal aging and permadeath system: every death permanently ages your character, and pushing past age 52 without Dog Bane healing at the Witch's Hut risks losing your character entirely. The Moola economy revolves around 450 Moola card rolls at the abandoned town near Bridgerman, with Dog Bane fishing as the safest solo income route. The Jane Juliet Railgun cursed technique dominates mid-range PvP through extreme posture-breaking, while horse stamina and vitality stats determine your escape capability when clans flood a server. This wiki covers all verified mechanics for the 2026 Alpha, updated from community gameplay data and competitor analysis.
