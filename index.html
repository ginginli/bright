Welcome to the Frontier

Mankind in their expansion west have settled within Ridge. B Valley. But this is no ordinary frontier.

Beasts hunt and forces beyond mortal reach skulk these grounds, their chaos trapped and anchored by those who came long before them. Your place within the valley is yet to be decided, but whatever path you choose to walk down, danger remains a constant.

"Forces beyond mortal reach skulk these grounds..."

Survival Mechanics

Visceral Combat

Master your firearms and your fists. Switch seamlessly between aiming down sights, reloading under pressure, and brutal blocking when enemies get too close.

Mount System

The valley is vast. Summon your horse, mount up, and traverse the grueling wilderness faster. But remember, a horse won't save you from the beasts that hunt.

Ocular Prowess

Unleash mysterious abilities beyond standard weaponry. Tap into the ancient power anchored in the valley to survive what hunts you.

Basic Controls

Movement

  • Q - Quick dash
  • W+W - Sprint
  • H - Summon/Unsummon horse
  • N - Ride horse/Dismount

Combat

  • M1 / M2 - Fire / Aim in
  • R / G - Reload / Melee
  • C / F - Fists / Block
  • X - Ocular Prowess

Menus

  • M - Statistics Menu

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the game finished?

No, the game is currently in Early Alpha. This means everything is a work in progress and subject to change as development continues.

What platforms can I play on?

Bridger: WESTERN is an experience built exclusively on Roblox, accessible via PC.

What about false anti-cheat bans?

The developers highly recommend having a clipping software running (such as Medal, Shadowplay, etc.) so you have video proof if you need to appeal a ban from the anti-cheat system.
