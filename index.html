Core Mechanics

🩸 Posture & Limb Damage

Damage is limb-specific. Constantly blocking (F) against fast attacks will shatter your posture, leaving you open to execution combos. Always wear a Hat to prevent headshots.

Learn more →

🌟 Stands & Corpse Parts

There are no Stand Arrows in Ridge B Valley. Stands are obtained exclusively through Corpse Part KOTH events. Use Root to reset an unwanted Stand.

💰 Moola Economy

Card rolls cost 450 Moola each at the abandoned town near Bridgerman. Dog Bane fishing is the safest solo income route. Co-op farming doubles efficiency.

⏳ Aging & Permadeath

Death permanently ages your avatar. Push past age 52 without Dog Bane healing at the Witch's Hut and you risk losing your character entirely.

About Bridger: WESTERN

Bridger: WESTERN is an Early Alpha Roblox survival game set in the cursed wilderness of Ridge B Valley. Players face permadeath aging mechanics, limb-based firearms combat, and supernatural threats. The game blends classic western gunfighting with paranormal abilities known as Ocular Prowess and JoJo-inspired Stands obtained through Corpse Part events.

Combat revolves around mastering guns and melee simultaneously. Weapons include the Winchester Repeater, Russian Roulette, Tommy Gun, Colt Ocelot, and Double Barrel Shotgun — each with distinct damage profiles and reload timings. The Posture system and Block (F) mechanic reward defensive play, while the Cards system (450 Moola per roll at Bridgerman) adds passive build customization for human characters.

The horse mount system is essential for traversing Ridge B Valley. Horses have independent stamina and vitality stats, summoned with H. Three factions — Outlaws, Inlaws, and Vampires — define your progression path. Vampires cannot use the human Card system. Bridger: WESTERN is free-to-play on Roblox PC, actively updated through 2026 Alpha.
