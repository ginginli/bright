About Bridger Western

Bridger Western is an Early Alpha Roblox survival game set in the cursed wilderness of Ridge B Valley. Bridger Western players face permadeath aging mechanics, limb-based firearms combat, and supernatural threats. Bridger Western blends classic western gunfighting with paranormal abilities known as Bridger Western Ocular Prowess and JoJo-inspired Bridger Western Stands obtained through Bridger Western Corpse Part events.

Bridger Western combat revolves around mastering Bridger Western guns and melee simultaneously. Bridger Western weapons include the Winchester Repeater, Russian Roulette, Tommy Gun, Colt Ocelot, and Double Barrel Shotgun — each with distinct damage profiles and reload timings. The Bridger Western Posture system and Bridger Western Block (F) mechanic reward defensive play, while the Bridger Western Cards system (450 Moola per roll at Bridgerman) adds passive build customization for human characters.

The Bridger Western horse mount system is essential for traversing Ridge B Valley. Bridger Western horses have independent stamina and vitality stats, summoned with H. Three factions — Bridger Western Outlaws, Bridger Western Inlaws, and Bridger Western Vampires — define your Bridger Western progression path. Bridger Western Vampires cannot use the human Card system. Bridger Western is free-to-play on Roblox PC, actively updated through 2026 Alpha.
