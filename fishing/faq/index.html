Frequently Asked Questions

📊 Drop Rates & Probabilities

What is the Arrow Shard drop rate?

Arrow Shards have a 0.1% drop rate (Legendary rarity).

This means approximately 1 in 1000 fishing attempts will yield an Arrow Shard on average.

Reality Check: With 0.1% drop rate, expect to fish for many hours before getting an Arrow Shard. This is the grindiest but safest Stand acquisition method.

How rare is Golden Bass?

Golden Bass has the same 0.1% drop rate as Arrow Shards (Legendary).

Value: 15,000 Moola when sold

Golden Bass is the most valuable single fishing item, worth 333x more than regular Bass ($45).

What are all the fishing drop rates?

Common (81.7%), Rare chests (16.25%), Epic Mares Leg (2%), Legendary (0.1%) — Golden Bass and Arrow Shards.

🎣 Getting Started

Where do I buy fishing gear?

Location: Tackle Bait | Fishing Goods

Prices:

  • Fishing Rod: 150 Moola
  • Bait: 15 Moola per pack

Do I need bait to fish?

No, but bait makes fishing 2x faster.

You can fish without bait, but having bait equipped makes pull time 2x faster.

How do I fish?

  1. Equip fishing rod near any water source
  2. Click and hold to cast bait
  3. Wait for fish to bite (screen indicator)
  4. Perform QTE (Quick Time Event) when prompted
  5. Successfully complete QTE to reel in catch

🌟 Arrow Shard Questions

Can I store Arrow Shards?

No. Arrow Shards cannot be banked or stored.

Arrow Shard Restrictions:

  • • Cannot be banked/stored
  • • Cannot be dropped or traded
  • • Cannot be used if you already have a Stand
Note: Arrow Shards cannot be banked or stored, and cannot be dropped. They are not lost upon death or leaving the server — you can safely keep fishing and use it when ready.

→ Full Arrow Shard Guide

What happens if I already have a Stand?

You cannot use Arrow Shards if you already possess a Stand.

Arrow Shards are only usable by players without Stands. If you fish up an Arrow Shard while having a Stand, it's wasted.

How long does it take to get an Arrow Shard?

Average: ~1000 fishing attempts (many hours of grinding).

With 0.1% drop rate, you need approximately 1000 successful catches on average. Actual time varies based on:

  • Whether you use bait (2x faster with bait)
  • Your QTE success rate
  • Luck (RNG can be brutal or generous)

💰 Moola Farming

Is fishing good for Moola farming?

Yes, fishing is one of the safest Moola farming methods.

Moola per fish:

  • Cod: $35
  • Snapper: $25
  • Bass: $45
  • Golden Bass: $15,000 (extremely rare)

Advantages:

  • Very safe (no PvP risk)
  • Consistent income from common fish
  • Chance at Golden Bass jackpot
  • Can fish for Arrow Shards simultaneously

What's the best fishing spot?

All water sources have the same drop rates.

There is no "best" fishing spot for legendary drops. Choose fishing locations based on:

  • Safety (low player traffic areas)
  • Proximity to Tackle Bait shop (for bait refills)
  • Personal preference

🔥 Fish Cooking

How do I cook fish?

Place fish in a campfire to cook them.

Cooked fish provide various buffs and healing effects when consumed.

What do cooked fish do?

Different cooked fish provide different effects.

Effects vary by fish type and can include healing, buffs, and other benefits useful for combat and survival.
