🎣 Getting Started

Location: Tackle Bait | Fishing Goods

How to Fish:

  1. Equip fishing rod near water
  2. Click and hold to cast bait

Bridger Western Fishing Wiki & Trello - Complete 2026 Drop Rate Guide

Bridger Western fishing is the primary Moola farming method and the only guaranteed solo path to obtaining Stands through Arrow Shard fishing (0.05% drop rate). This comprehensive Bridger Western fishing guide covers all drop rates, fishing mechanics, and optimal strategies for Ridge B Valley. Unlike other Bridger Western Trello fishing guides, we provide verified drop rate data: Common fish (81.7%), Rare chests (16.25%), Epic Mares Leg (2%), and Legendary drops (0.05%) including Golden Bass and Arrow Shards.

The Bridger Western Arrow Shard fishing method requires purchasing a fishing rod (150 Moola) and bait (15 Moola) from Tackle Bait | Fishing Goods. With a 0.05% drop rate, players need approximately 2000 fishing attempts on average to obtain a single Arrow Shard. Bridger Western Golden Bass fishing shares the same 0.05% legendary drop rate but sells for 15,000 Moola, making it the most valuable single item for Moola farming. Common fish provide steady income: Cod ($35), Snapper ($25), and Bass ($45) can be sold for consistent Moola generation.

Bridger Western fishing locations include any water source across the map, with no specific "best spot" for legendary drops. The Bridger Western fishing mechanics involve casting bait, waiting for a bite, and completing a QTE (Quick Time Event) to reel in the catch. Having bait equipped makes pull time 2x faster, significantly improving fishing efficiency. Bridger Western fish cooking adds another layer — cooked fish provide various buffs and healing effects when consumed, making them valuable for combat preparation and KOTH event survival.
