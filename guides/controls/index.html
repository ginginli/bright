Controls & Keybinds
Complete Input Reference

All controls for Bridger Western — movement, combat, horse riding, and special abilities. Quick reference table and detailed explanations.

On This Page

⌨️ All Controls — Quick Reference

🏃 Movement

  • W/A/S/D — Move
  • Double-tap W — Sprint
  • Q — Roll/dodge
  • Sprint — Auto-vaults obstacles

⚔️ Combat

  • M1 (Left Click) — Shoot
  • Right Click — Aim down sights
  • R — Reload
  • G — Melee with weapon
  • C — Switch to fists

🐴 Horse

  • H — Summon/dismiss horse
  • N — Mount/dismount
  • Shift — Sprint on horse
  • Space — Jump (hold = high jump)
  • F — Horse attack / full stop
  • M — View horse stats

👁️ Special

  • X — Ocular Pro S
  • M — View stats/age/tier
  • Z — Aim down sights (toggle)

🏃 Movement Controls

Sprint & Auto-Vault

Double-tap W to sprint. While sprinting, your character automatically vaults over fences and small obstacles — no extra input needed.

Roll / Dodge

Press Q to roll. Use this to dodge incoming attacks during combat.

Tip: Sprint + auto-vault is essential for navigating Ridge B Valley quickly. Practice sprinting through fences to build muscle memory.

⚔️ Combat Controls

Ranged Combat

  • • M1 — Fire weapon
  • • Right Click — Aim down sights (better accuracy)
  • • R — Reload
  • • Z — Toggle ADS

Melee Combat

  • • G — Quick melee with equipped weapon
  • • C — Switch to fists when out of ammo
Tip: G melee is fast and can interrupt enemy actions. Use it to finish off low-health enemies without wasting ammo.

🐴 Horse Controls

Basic Horse Controls

  • • H — Summon or dismiss your horse
  • • N — Mount or dismount
  • • Shift — Sprint while mounted
  • • Space — Jump (hold for higher jump)
  • • F — Horse kick attack (significant damage)
  • • F — Full stop while sprinting
  • • M — View horse stats (Speed, Stamina, Jump, Damage, Scale, Turn)

🌊 Horse Water Tech

Cross water quickly with this technique:

  1. Mount horse (N)
  2. Sprint (hold Shift)
  3. Enter water while sprinting
  4. Immediately hold Space

Essential for reaching the Mud Witch in the swamp.

Note: If your horse dies, it takes approximately 2 minutes to respawn. Avoid unnecessary combat while mounted.

👁️ Ocular Pro S

Activation: Press X

Ocular Pro S is a special ability that enhances combat performance when activated.

  • • Enemy Highlighting — Shows enemy positions
  • • Accuracy Boost — Improves weapon accuracy
  • • Reload Speed — Increases reload speed
  • • Aimbot (with cards) — Certain card combinations enable aimbot functionality
Card Combo: High Noon + Evil Eye cards enable aimbot with Ocular Pro S. See Card Builds →

Bridger Western Controls & Keybinds Guide – Complete Reference 2026

Bridger Western controls cover movement (WASD, double-tap W to sprint, Q to roll), combat (M1 to shoot, Right Click to aim, R to reload, G for melee, C for fists), horse riding (H to summon, N to mount, Shift to sprint, Space to jump, F for attack/stop), and special abilities (X for Ocular Pro S). The Bridger Western keybinds are straightforward but mastering advanced techniques like horse water tech (Shift + Space in water) is essential for efficient traversal.

Bridger Western horse controls are particularly important — press M while mounted to view your horse's stats (Speed, Stamina, Jump, Damage, Scale, Turn). The Bridger Western Ocular Pro S (activated with X) provides enemy highlighting, accuracy boost, and reload speed increase. Combined with specific cards like High Noon and Evil Eye, it enables aimbot functionality. See Card Builds for optimal setups.

For Bridger Western Stand controls, each Stand uses different keybinds (E, R, T, Y, F, G, H). See the Stand Keybinds Guide for the complete reference. New players should start with the Beginner Guide before diving into advanced controls.
