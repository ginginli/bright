🏃 Movement Controls

  • W/A/S/D - Move forward/left/backward/right
  • Double-tap W - Sprint (auto-vaults obstacles)
  • Q - Roll/dodge
  • Hold Shift - Sprint while on horse

⚔️ Combat Controls

  • M1 (Left Click) - Shoot weapon
  • Right Click - Aim down sights
  • R - Reload weapon
  • G - Melee attack with weapon
  • C - Switch to fists (when out of ammo)

🐎 Horse Controls

  • H - Summon/dismiss horse
  • N - Mount/dismount horse
  • F - Hit with horse (does significant damage)
  • Space - Jump on horse (hold for high jump)
  • M - View horse stats while mounted

👁️ Special Abilities

  • X - Ocular Pro S (ESP/aimbot activation)
  • M - View talents, age, alignment, XP, tier
  • F - Full stop while sprinting on horse

Detailed Control Explanations

🏃 Movement & Mobility

"Now we have the first one is we have double tap W to sprint. You will automatically vault obstacles right here. Take a look at that. Now we have Q to roll like other games out there." - Guide Video 1

Key Movement Techniques:

  • Double-tap W Sprint: Most basic sprint method, automatically vaults over small obstacles while sprinting
  • Q Roll: Standard dodge/roll mechanic for avoiding attacks
  • Auto-vaulting: While sprinting, your character automatically climbs over fences and small obstacles

⚔️ Weapon & Combat Controls

"The next step is with your weapon. We have M1 to shoot. Right click to aim and then shoot. And then basically we have R to reload. So yeah, take a look at that, see? You could also press G to melee someone with your weapon on." - Guide Video 1

Combat Techniques:

  • M1 Shooting: Primary fire button for all weapons
  • Right Click Aiming: Aim down sights for better accuracy
  • R Reload: Standard reload for all weapons
  • G Melee: Quick melee attack with your equipped weapon
  • C Fists: Switch to hand-to-hand combat when out of ammo

🐎 Horse Mechanics & Controls

"The last one to summon your horse, just press H on your keyboard. Uh yep. Uh where is it? Okay, there we go. Now hold shift to sprint. And then if you're if you want to full stop right away while sprinting, you can press F like this. Look at that, see? And then just press N again to dismount, okay?" - Guide Video 1

Horse Control Details:

  • H to Summon: Calls your horse to your location
  • N to Mount/Dismount: Primary horse interaction key
  • Shift Sprint: Makes horse run at full speed
  • F Horse Attack: Horse kicks forward, dealing significant damage
  • Space Jump: Press once for small jump, hold for high jump
  • Horse Death: If horse dies, takes ~2 minutes to respawn

👁️ Ocular Pro S (ESP/Aimbot System)

"And then if you press X, you will use Ocular Pro S. It will grant you better accuracy, highlight people right here and then also increases your reload speed and more. With certain cards right here, it will allow you to use aimbot as well with Ocular Pro S." - Guide Video 1

Ocular Pro S Features:

  • X Activation: Toggles Ocular Pro S on/off
  • Enemy Highlighting: Shows enemy positions through walls
  • Accuracy Boost: Improves weapon accuracy while active
  • Reload Speed: Increases reload speed
  • Aimbot Potential: Certain cards enable aimbot functionality with Ocular Pro S

Advanced Control Techniques

🚀 Horse Water Tech

"Let me show you guys this horse tech, bro. Like hold shift and then once you go into the water, just hold space. Like yeah, you can actually travel the water with horse fast. Yeah, this is very useful tech, bro." - Guide Video 1

How to Perform:

  1. Mount your horse (press N)
  2. Start sprinting (hold Shift)
  3. Enter water while sprinting
  4. Immediately hold Space bar

Use Cases: Escaping pursuers, reaching swamp area quickly, crossing rivers safely.

⚡ Quick Stop Technique

"And then if you're if you want to full stop right away while sprinting, you can press F like this. Look at that, see?" - Guide Video 1

How to Perform:

  1. Be mounted on horse
  2. Sprinting (holding Shift)
  3. Press F to perform immediate stop

Use Cases: Quick combat dismounts, precise positioning, avoiding obstacles.
