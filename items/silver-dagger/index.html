🗡️ Silver Dagger Guide

The last line of defense against vampires and humans when you're out of ammo

⚔️ Quick Stats

🗡️ Basic Attack

  • Base Damage: 8 per swing
  • vs Vampires: 16 damage (double)
  • Effect: Leaves silver fire on vampires
  • Cost: Consumes stamina

🤜 Grab Attack (M2)

  • Total Damage: 28 damage
  • vs Vampires: 56 damage (double)
  • Animation: Grab → Shank → Kick away
  • Range: Forward grab

⚔️ Combat Guide

🗡️ Basic Attacks

The Silver Dagger's primary attack is a forward slash that deals 8 base damage to humans and 16 damage to vampires (automatic double damage). Each swing consumes stamina, so manage your energy carefully during extended fights.

🔥 Silver Fire Effect

When attacking vampires, the Silver Dagger leaves silver fire on the target, dealing additional damage over time. This makes it particularly effective against vampire players and provides a significant advantage in vampire encounters.

🤜 Grab Attack (M2)

Press M2 (right-click) to perform a devastating grab attack:

  1. Grab: Lunges forward to grab the target
  2. Shank: Stabs the grabbed enemy multiple times
  3. Kick: Kicks the enemy away to finish the combo

This combo deals 28 total damage to humans and 56 damage to vampires, making it one of the most powerful melee attacks in the game.

🃏 Card Synergies

The Silver Dagger's damage can be significantly increased with certain cards:

🥈 Silver Prodigy

  • Increases silver weapon damage
  • Enhances silver fire effect
  • Perfect synergy with Silver Dagger

👊 Boy With Fists

  • Increases melee damage
  • Boosts both swing and grab attacks
  • Stacks with Silver Prodigy

Damage Calculation: With both cards equipped, your Silver Dagger can deal significantly more than the base 8/28 damage, making it a viable primary weapon even in late-game scenarios.

💡 Usage Tips

🎯 When to Use

  • Out of Ammo: Emergency backup when guns are empty
  • Vampire Encounters: Highly effective against vampire players
  • Close Quarters: Excellent for tight spaces where guns are impractical
  • Stealth Kills: Silent takedowns without alerting others

⚡ Combat Strategy

  • Stamina Management: Don't spam attacks - manage your stamina
  • Grab Timing: Use M2 grab when enemy is distracted or stunned
  • Hit and Run: Attack and retreat to avoid counterattacks
  • Vampire Priority: Focus vampires first due to double damage

⚠️ Limitations

  • Range: Melee only - vulnerable to ranged attacks
  • Stamina Cost: Can't spam attacks indefinitely
  • Animation Lock: Grab attack has long animation
  • No Block: Cannot defend against incoming attacks

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How much damage does the Silver Dagger do?

The Silver Dagger deals 8 base damage per swing to humans and 16 damage to vampires (automatic double damage). The grab attack (M2) deals 28 damage to humans and 56 damage to vampires.

What is the silver fire effect?

When attacking vampires, the Silver Dagger leaves silver fire on the target, dealing additional damage over time. This effect only works on vampires, not humans.

Which cards work best with the Silver Dagger?

Silver Prodigy and Boy With Fists are the best cards for Silver Dagger builds. Silver Prodigy enhances silver weapon damage, while Boy With Fists increases melee damage. Both effects stack.

How do I perform the grab attack?

Press M2 (right-click) to perform a grab attack. This lunges forward, grabs the target, shanks them multiple times, then kicks them away for 28 total damage (56 vs vampires).

Does the Silver Dagger consume stamina?

Yes, both the basic slash attack and the grab attack consume stamina. Manage your stamina carefully during fights to avoid being left defenseless.

Is the Silver Dagger effective against humans?

While it deals normal damage to humans (8 per swing, 28 for grab), it's most effective against vampires due to the double damage and silver fire effect. However, it's still a viable backup weapon when out of ammo.
