🌿 Rare Items FAQ

What is Dogbane Herb and how do I get it?

Dogbane Herb is a rare 4% chance drop from chests.

Uses at Mud Witch:

  • Trade for 2500 Moola
  • Age Regression (-20 years)
  • Stand Removal
Tip: Save Dogbane Herb for stand removal if you got an unwanted stand like Tusk.

How does Silver Dagger work?

Silver Dagger drops from chests and is a powerful vampire-hunting tool.

  • Primary Attack: Slashes forward, takes stamina
  • Damage: 8 base damage (double to vampires = 16 damage)
  • Special Effect: Leaves silver fire on vampires
  • M2 Attack: Grab and shank

What are the best chest farming locations?

Chests spawn in various locations around the map.

Chests can contain:

  • Dogbane Herb (4% chance)
  • Silver Dagger
  • Other valuable items

Check buildings, caves, and hidden areas for chest spawns.

💣 Combat Items FAQ

How does the Dynamite + Smoke Bomb combo work?

Tossing a molotov on a smoke bomb creates a large explosion.

Combo steps:

  1. Throw smoke bomb to create smoke screen
  2. Throw molotov into the smoke
  3. Large explosion occurs
Warning: This combo can damage you too. Keep distance after throwing.

Do Outlaws get more dynamite?

Yes, Outlaws throw multiple dynamite based on tier:

  • Tier 1: 1 dynamite
  • Tier 2: 2 dynamite
  • Tier 3: 3 dynamite

This makes Outlaws significantly more dangerous with explosives at higher tiers.

How does the Coin ricochet work?

Coin is inspired by Ultrakill's ricochet mechanic.

How to use:

  1. Toss coin into the air
  2. Wait for the glimmer effect
  3. Shoot the coin during glimmer
  4. Hitscan shot deflects to nearest entity

Requires precise timing but can hit enemies behind cover.

What's the difference between Dynamite and Molotov?

Different purposes and effects:

Item Effect Best Use
Dynamite Explodes after delay Direct damage, destroying cover
Molotov Leaves fire pool Area denial, blocking paths
Smoke Bomb Creates smoke screen Escape, combo with molotov

🗡️ Vampire Hunting FAQ

How does Silver Ammo work?

Silver Ammo applies silver damage to vampires.

Silver Prodigy Card Synergy:

  • Possessing [Silver Prodigy] card spawns you with +1 silver ammo pack each life
  • Permanent advantage for vampire hunting builds

Silver damage is essential for effectively fighting vampires.

How long does Wooden Stake last?

Wooden Stake lasts approximately 10 seconds.

Effects:

  • Leaves stake embedded in vampire
  • Burns vampire while stake is in them (~10s)
  • Emergency anti-vampire solution

Use when caught off-guard by vampires without silver weapons.

Does Steel Wireset work on vampires?

Yes, with the [Silver Wireset] card.

How it works:

  • Click to place origin point
  • Click again to send wire forth
  • With [Silver Wireset] card: Gains silver burn to vampires

Without the card, it works as a normal trap/wire.

🍾 Consumables FAQ

What happens if I drink too much Tonic?

Drinking too much Tonic causes screen blur and pass out.

Effects:

  • Heals small amount of HP per drink
  • Screen gets more blurred with each drink
  • Eventually you pass out
Warning: Don't spam tonic during combat. The blur effect can get you killed.

How many arrows does Arrow Pack give?

Arrow Pack gives 5 arrows per pack.

Essential for bow users. Stock up before long fights or exploration.

Where can I buy Knives?

Knives can be bought nearly all around the map.

Look for vendors in towns and settlements. Knives are basic throwing weapons available almost everywhere.

👔 Wearables FAQ

Is Poncho worth the limb damage trade-off?

Depends on your playstyle.

Poncho effects:

  • ✅ Take less overall damage
  • ❌ Take more limb damage
  • ✅ Makes you look swag

Best for: Players who avoid headshots and focus on body shots. Not recommended if you're frequently targeted by snipers.

Does Cowboy Hat respawn after blocking a headshot?

No, Cowboy Hat is consumed after blocking 1 headshot.

You'll need to get a new hat after it saves you from a headshot. Always keep spare hats if possible.
