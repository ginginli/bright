🍈 How Rokakaka Fruit Works

⚠️ Important Bug Warning

Rokakakas cannot be dropped, but are kept upon death and can be stored in bank. There is a bug that consumes multiple Rokakakas at once upon single use — make sure to store them for individual use.

How to Use

Upon obtaining a Rokakaka, you may click on a player to prompt them to "Split" the Rokakaka with you. Both players must agree to proceed.

What Splitting Does

Splitting a Rokakaka with another player performs a set of actions:

  • Equalizing your ages
  • Swapping 1–2 cards
  • Trading stands (optional for the player clicked)
  • Turning base stands into a random shiny variant when trading — 1% chance

📋 Requirements

  • Both players must be Tier 3
  • Both players must possess a stand
  • The other player must agree to the split

🏦 Storage Rules

  • Cannot be dropped
  • Kept upon death
  • Can be stored in bank
  • Store individually to avoid the multi-consume bug

Bridger Western Rokakaka Fruit – Complete 2026 Guide

The Bridger Western Rokakaka Fruit is a special item that allows two Tier 3 players who both possess a stand to split the fruit together. Bridger Western stand trading is done through the Rokakaka — click on a player to prompt them to split it with you. The split action equalizes both players' ages, swaps 1–2 cards, and optionally trades stands. There is also a 1% chance to turn a base stand into a random shiny variant when trading stands via Rokakaka. The fruit cannot be dropped but is kept upon death and can be stored in the bank. Due to a known bug, store Rokakakas individually to avoid multiple being consumed at once.
