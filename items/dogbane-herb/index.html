🌿 Dogbane Herb – Key Facts

📊 Item Properties

  • 📦 Source: Chests (found around the map)
  • 📉 Drop Rate: 4% (Rare)
  • 🧙 Used at: Mud Witch in the Swamp
  • 🌿 Type: Consumable / Trade item

⚠️ Important Notes

  • 💡 Very rare — save it for the right moment
  • 🌟 Best use: Stand removal or age regression
  • 💰 Least valuable: Trading for 2500 Moola
  • 🎯 Plan ahead before trading

📦 How to Get Dogbane Herb

Dogbane Herb drops from chests found around the map with a 4% drop chance. Open chests whenever you find them — the herb is rare but not impossible to obtain with regular play.

Tip: Dogbane Herb is rare (4% chest drop). Don't waste it — plan what you need before trading it to the Mud Witch.

🧙 Uses at the Mud Witch

Take the Dogbane Herb to the Mud Witch in the Swamp and choose one of three options:

💰 Option 1: 2500 Moola

Trade the herb for 2500 Moola.

⚠️ Least recommended. 2500 Moola can be earned through fishing. Save the herb for more valuable uses.

⏳ Option 2: Age Regression (-20 Years)

Reverse your character's age by 20 years.

Every death ages your character. If you combat log, age increases by 10. Age regression is essential for long-term survival.

✅ Recommended for players who die frequently or are approaching old age.

🌟 Option 3: Stand Removal

Remove your current Stand.

Useful if you obtained an unwanted Stand — especially Tusk, which permanently disables walking and weapon use.

✅ Recommended if you got Tusk or another Stand you don't want.

🎯 When to Use Each Option

Situation Best Option
Got Tusk (can't walk or use weapons) 🌟 Stand Removal
Got an unwanted Stand from Arrow Shard 🌟 Stand Removal
Character is getting very old ⏳ Age Regression
Dying frequently, age rising fast ⏳ Age Regression
Happy with Stand, young character 💰 2500 Moola (last resort)

❓ Dogbane Herb FAQ

What is Dogbane Herb used for?

Dogbane Herb is traded to the Mud Witch in the Swamp for one of three options: 2500 Moola, age regression (-20 years), or Stand removal.

How rare is Dogbane Herb?

Dogbane Herb has a 4% drop chance from chests. It is a Rare item — not extremely rare, but uncommon enough that you should plan how to use it before trading.

Where is the Mud Witch?

The Mud Witch is located in the Swamp area. Use the horse water technique (sprint on horse + hold Space in water) to reach her hut quickly. See the Locations Guide for details.

Should I use Dogbane Herb for Moola?

Generally no. 2500 Moola can be earned through fishing relatively quickly. Age regression and Stand removal are much harder to obtain otherwise, making them far more valuable uses for the herb.

Can I remove Tusk with Dogbane Herb?

Yes. Stand removal via Dogbane Herb works on all Stands including Tusk. This is the recommended use if you accidentally obtained Tusk, which permanently disables walking and weapon use. See the Stands FAQ for more on Tusk.
