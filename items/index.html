Bridger Western Items Database

Items & Utilities
Complete Guide

Master all items and utilities in Ridge B Valley. Consumables, throwables, special equipment, and vampire-hunting tools.

💊 Consumables & Ammo

📦

Ammo Box

Replenishes ammo for your weapons. Essential for extended combat.

✨

Silver Ammo

Applies silver damage to vampires. Possessing the [Silver Prodigy] card spawns you with +1 silver ammo pack each life.

🍾

Tonic

Roblox friendly alcohol. Drink to heal a small amount of HP. The more you drink, the more blurred your screen gets until you pass out.

🌿

Dogbane Herb

Rare 4%

Rare 4% chance from chests. Can be brought to the Mud Witch for 2500 moola, age regression (-20 years), or a stand removal.

🏹

Arrow Pack

For all your bow shooting needs. Gives 5 arrows per pack.

🔪

Knives

Can be bought nearly all around the map. Basic throwing weapon.

💣 Throwables & Explosives

💥

Dynamite

Throw dynamite, explodes after a little. Outlaws throw 2 at tier 2 and 3 at tier 3.

🔥

Molotov

Leaves a fire pool on impact. Area denial weapon.

💨

Smoke Bomb

Leaves a smoke screen. Tossing a molotov on it creates a large explosion.

🪙

Coin

Totally not a ricochet from Ultrakill. Toss into the air, shooting it during the glimmer deflects a hitscan shot to the nearest entity.

⚔️ Special Equipment

🗡️

Silver Dagger

Chest Drop

Drops from chests. Slashes forward and takes stamina, leaves silver fire on vampires. Does 8 base damage (double to vampires). M2 does a grab and shank.

🔱

Wooden Stake

Emergency anti vampire solution. Leaves a stake in them for ~10s, burns them while it's in them.

🪢

Steel Wireset

Click to place origin, click again to send it forth. Gains silver burn to vampires if accompanied by the [Silver Wireset] card.

🪢

Lasso

Used for tying up wrong doers and evil folk.

👔 Wearables & Accessories

🧥

Poncho

Makes you look swag. Makes you take less overall damage in exchange for taking more limb damage.

🤠

Cowboy Hat

Protects you from 1 headshot. Essential survival item.

🏮

Lantern

Provides light to your surroundings. Useful for night exploration.

🌟 Key Items & Rare Drops

Dogbane Herb (4% Chest Drop)

Uses at Mud Witch:

  • 2500 Moola
  • Age Regression (-20 years)
  • Stand Removal

Silver Prodigy Card Synergy

Silver Ammo Bonus:

  • Spawn with +1 silver ammo pack each life
  • Permanent vampire-hunting advantage

The Bridger Western coin is a ricochet mechanic inspired by Ultrakill: toss it into the air and shoot it during the glimmer to deflect a hitscan shot to the nearest entity. Bridger Western steel wireset requires clicking to place origin, then clicking again to send it forth — gains silver burn to vampires if accompanied by the Silver Wireset card. Bridger Western poncho makes you take less overall damage in exchange for taking more limb damage, while the Bridger Western cowboy hat protects you from 1 headshot. Bridger Western lasso is used for tying up wrongdoers and evil folk.
