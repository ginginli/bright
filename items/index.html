Bridger Western Items & Utilities Wiki & Trello – Complete 2026 Database

Looking for the complete Bridger Western items guide? This database covers all Bridger Western consumables, Bridger Western throwables, and Bridger Western utilities. The Bridger Western Dogbane herb is a rare 4% chest drop that can be traded to the Mud Witch for 2500 Moola, age regression (-20 years), or stand removal. Bridger Western silver ammo applies silver damage to vampires, and possessing the Silver Prodigy card spawns you with +1 silver ammo pack each life. Bridger Western tonic is Roblox-friendly alcohol that heals a small amount of HP, but drinking too much blurs your screen until you pass out.

Bridger Western dynamite is a throwable explosive that detonates after a short delay. Outlaws throw 2 dynamite at tier 2 and 3 at tier 3. Bridger Western molotov leaves a fire pool on impact for area denial, while Bridger Western smoke bomb creates a smoke screen — tossing a molotov on smoke creates a large explosion. The Bridger Western coin is a ricochet mechanic inspired by Ultrakill: toss it into the air and shoot it during the glimmer to deflect a hitscan shot to the nearest entity. The Bridger Western Silver Dagger is the ultimate anti-vampire melee weapon, dealing 8 base damage (16 to vampires) with silver fire effects and a devastating 28-damage grab attack.

Bridger Western wooden stake is an emergency anti-vampire solution that leaves a stake in vampires for ~10 seconds, burning them while it's embedded. Bridger Western steel wireset requires clicking to place origin, then clicking again to send it forth — gains silver burn to vampires if accompanied by the Silver Wireset card. Bridger Western poncho makes you take less overall damage in exchange for taking more limb damage, while the Bridger Western cowboy hat protects you from 1 headshot. Bridger Western lasso is used for tying up wrongdoers and evil folk. Understanding Bridger Western item mechanics and Bridger Western utility usage is essential for survival in Ridge B Valley's harsh frontier environment.
