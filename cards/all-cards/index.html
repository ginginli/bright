Bridger Western All Cards List - Complete 2026 Database

This Bridger Western all cards list contains all 25 Tarot Cards available in the game. The Bridger Western cards database is organized by category: Ranged Combat cards (Archer's Child, Desperado), Melee Combat cards (Boy With Fists, Belmont Family Crest, Show Me A Good Time), Horseback Combat cards (A True Cowboy), Explosives cards (Demolitions Expert), Mobility cards (Free Runner, Poltergeist), High Risk/High Reward cards (Can't Lay Off the Tonic, Flesh Automaton, High Noon, Snake Eater), and Special Abilities cards (Quick Draw, Evil Eye, EXECUTIONER).

The Bridger Western Archer's Child card is the best option for bow builds with 1.5x drawspeed and 2x firerate. Bridger Western Desperado card provides massive gun damage at low HP, making it ideal for aggressive playstyles. Bridger Western Free Runner card is universally useful with +20 stamina and slide ability. Bridger Western EXECUTIONER card enables instant kills via headshot decapitation (40+ damage threshold). Bridger Western Boy With Fists card grants +20% melee damage but reduces gun accuracy by 55%, perfect for pure melee builds.

There are 9 undocumented Bridger Western cards that are available in-game but lack detailed descriptions: Secret Technique, Silver Prodigy, STURDY, Time Heals All Wounds, Total Checkmate, Twenty Meter Radius Steel, Veteran, and Winged Man. The Bridger Western Coin Roller card is currently disabled and cannot be obtained. All cards are obtained at the Mud Witch in Swamp for 150 Moola, with a 3-card maximum per character. Players choose 1 card from 3 random options per roll.
