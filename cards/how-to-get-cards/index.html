Card Acquisition Guide

How to Get Cards
Complete Guide

Everything you need to know about obtaining Tarot Cards at the Mud Witch.

📍 Basic Information

Location

NPC: Mud Witch

Area: Swamp

The Mud Witch is located in the Swamp area. Look for the witch's hut near the water.

Cost & Limits

Cost: 150 Moola per roll

Selection: Choose 1 out of 3 cards

Max Cards: 3 cards per character

🎴 How Card Rolling Works

  1. Visit the Mud Witch in the Swamp area
  2. Pay 150 Moola to roll for cards
  3. 3 random cards will be presented to you
  4. Choose 1 card from the 3 options
  5. Repeat until you have 3 cards total

Important: You can only hold 3 cards maximum. Once you have 3 cards, you cannot roll for more unless you remove an existing card.

🎯 Card Priority Guide

🏆 High Priority Cards

  • Archer's Child - Essential for bow builds
  • Free Runner - Universal mobility boost
  • EXECUTIONER - Instant kill potential
  • Quick Draw - Combat versatility
  • Desperado - High damage potential

⚠️ Situational Cards

  • Boy With Fists - Only for pure melee
  • Belmont Family Crest - Only vs vampires
  • A True Cowboy - Only for mounted combat
  • High Noon - Timing-dependent
  • Poltergeist - High stamina cost

🔄 Reroll Strategy

When to Reroll

If you get 3 cards you don't want, you have two options:

Option 1: Keep Rolling

Wait until you see at least one good card in the 3-card selection before choosing.

Option 2: Remove Cards

If you already have 3 cards, you must remove one before rolling again.

Warning: Each roll costs 150 Moola. Budget accordingly and prioritize cards that fit your playstyle.

💰 Moola Farming for Cards

Fishing

Dog Bane fishing is the safest solo income route. Low risk, steady income.

Co-op Farming

Team up with other players to farm Moola faster. Doubles efficiency.

Budget Planning

Plan to spend 450-600 Moola total (3-4 rolls) to get your ideal 3-card setup.
