Bridger Western Cards Wiki & Guide - Complete 2026 Alpha Database

Looking for the Bridger Western cards guide? This wiki covers everything about Bridger Western Tarot Cards — all 25 cards, effects, and acquisition methods. The Bridger Western cards list includes powerful options like Archer's Child (1.5x bow drawspeed, 2x firerate), Desperado (more gun damage at low HP), Free Runner (+20 stamina, slide ability), and EXECUTIONER (headshot decapitation). Cards are obtained at the Mud Witch in the Swamp for 150 Moola per roll, with a maximum of 3 cards per character.

Bridger Western how to get cards is straightforward: visit the Mud Witch location in Swamp, pay 150 Moola, and choose 1 card from 3 random options. The Bridger Western card reroll strategy involves waiting for at least one good card in your 3-card selection before committing. Popular Bridger Western best cards include Archer's Child for bow builds, Quick Draw for gun builds, Boy With Fists for melee builds, and Free Runner as a universal mobility card. The Bridger Western 3 card limit means you must carefully plan your build — once you have 3 cards, you cannot roll for more without removing an existing card.

Bridger Western card builds vary by playstyle: bow builds use Archer's Child + EXECUTIONER + Free Runner for maximum ranged DPS; aggressive gun builds combine Desperado + Quick Draw + Poltergeist for high-risk combat; pure melee builds stack Boy With Fists + Show Me A Good Time + Free Runner for close-range dominance. The Bridger Western Mud Witch is the only NPC that sells cards, located in the Swamp area near the water. Budget 450-600 Moola total (3-4 rolls) to get your ideal 3-card setup. Some cards like High Noon (-10 Max HP, autoaim at noon) and Flesh Automaton (explode on death) are high-risk options for meme builds.
