📊 Horse Stats

How to View Horse Stats

Press M while mounted to see your horse's stats and name on the bottom left of the screen.

Horse Stats — Range: 0.85 to 1.25

  • • Speed: How fast your horse can run
  • • Stamina: How long your horse can sprint
  • • Jump: How high your horse can jump
  • • Damage: Combat damage dealt by horse
  • • Scale: Size of your horse
  • • Turn: How quickly your horse can turn
Stat range: All stats vary from 0.85 to 1.25. 【 MYTHICAL 】 and 「LEGENDARY」 horses can receive boosts that exceed the 1.25 limit.

🌟 Horse Rarities

Horse Rarity Tiers

Horses come in different rarities. 【 MYTHICAL 】 and 「LEGENDARY」 horses are identified by their brackets and FULL CAPS name. A confirmation prompt appears so you don't accidentally skip them.

Common — Base stats, low price
Rare — Improved stats
Epic — Good stats
「LEGENDARY」 — Receives 1 significant stat boost on any stat capable of exceeding the 1.25 limit. Does not guarantee good base stats.
【 MYTHICAL 】 — Receives 2 significant stat boosts on any stats capable of exceeding the 1.25 limit. Does not guarantee good base stats.
Note: Legendary and Mythical horses boost existing stats — they do not come with predetermined good stats. A medium-cost Legendary is only as good as its base stats.

📊 Price Formula

The price is determined by adding up all of your horse's stats:

High total → Expensive horse — described as "top of the line", "beast", "best runner", etc.
Mid total → Mid horse
Low total → Low horse

【 MYTHICAL 】 and 「LEGENDARY」 Horses

These special horses receive a significant stat boost on any stat capable of exceeding the 1.25 limit:

  • • 「LEGENDARY」 — gets 1 boost
  • • 【 MYTHICAL 】 — gets 2 boosts
Important: These horses do NOT have predetermined "good" stats. The boost applies to already existing stats. A medium-cost Legendary horse will only be as good as its base stats suggest.

How to identify them: Look for their corresponding brackets and FULL CAPS in the name. There is also a confirmation prompt that stops you from accidentally skipping over them.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best horse in Bridger Western?

A horse priced at 800 Moola and up is generally considered ideal. Price is determined by adding up all of the horse's stats — higher total stats = higher price.

【 MYTHICAL 】 and 「LEGENDARY」 horses receive extra stat boosts, but they do not have predetermined good stats. A medium-cost Legendary is only as good as its base stats suggest.

Tip: When you see a Legendary or Mythical horse, a confirmation prompt will appear so you don't accidentally skip it.

How do I check my horse stats?

Press M while mounted. Your horse's stats and name will appear on the bottom left of the screen.

Where can I replace my horse?

Visit Horse Stables at various locations across the map. The NPC will tell you a price, horse name, and vaguely describe the horse.

  • 800 Moola and up is generally the ideal range for good horses

Why do I need a good horse for Tusk Stand?

Tusk users cannot walk and need horses for mobility.

Tusk Stand restrictions:

  • Cannot walk (crawl only)
  • Cannot use weapons
  • Requires constant protection from teammates
Critical: A legendary or mythical horse becomes essential for Tusk mobility. Do you have a legendary/mythical horse before getting Tusk?

What is A True Cowboy card?

A horseback combat specialization card.

Card effects:

  • Your lasso auto-aims at other players while on horseback
  • Deal 17% more gun damage while mounted

Best for: Players who prefer mounted combat. Pairs well with gun builds and horse-focused playstyles.
