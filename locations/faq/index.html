Frequently Asked Questions

🧙 Mud Witch

Where is the Mud Witch?

Location: Swamp area (Mud Witch's Hut)

The Mud Witch is located in the Swamp region of the map. Look for her hut in the swampy terrain.

What does the Mud Witch do?

The Mud Witch offers two main services:

1. Read Your Fortune (Card Rolls):

  • Cost: 150 Moola per roll
  • Choose 1 card from 3 random options
  • Maximum 3 cards per character

2. Offer Dogbane Herb:

  • Get 2500 Moola
  • Decrease age by 20 years
  • Wipe/remove your Stand

What should I trade Dogbane Herb for?

It depends on your situation:

  • 2500 Moola: If you need money for cards or equipment
  • Age Regression (-20 years): If you're approaching age 52 (permadeath risk)
  • Stand Removal: If you want to reroll for a different Stand
Recommendation: Age regression is most valuable for long-term survival. Dogbane Herb is rare (4% chest drop).

🏪 Shops & Services

Where do I buy guns?

Gun Store in Main Town

The Gun Store sells most weapons in the game, including primary and secondary weapons (secondary requires Tier 3).

Where do I buy Cowboy Hat and Poncho?

General Store in Main Town

Available items:

  • Cowboy Hat (headshot protection)
  • Poncho (damage reduction, increased limb damage)
  • Lasso, Coin, Lantern, Knives

What does the Banker do?

The Banker provides item storage.

You can store:

  • Guns
  • Utilities
  • Items
Critical: All items in bank are NOT kept upon server wipe. Bank is temporary storage only.

How much does customization cost?

Tailor (Clothing):

  • Reroll Shirt & Pants: 15 Moola
  • Reroll Accessory: 30 Moola

Barbershop (Hair):

  • Reroll Hairstyle: 25 Moola
  • Reroll Hair Color: 15 Moola
Note: Customization is purely cosmetic and doesn't affect gameplay.

🐴 Horse Stables

Where are Horse Stables?

Multiple locations across the map

Horse Stables can be found in various locations. Look for "Horse Stables | Horse Info" NPCs.

How do I check my horse stats?

Press M to view horse stats

Horse Stats:

  • Speed
  • Stamina
  • Jump
  • Damage
  • Scale
  • Turn

What are the horse rarities?

Horse rarities from lowest to highest:

  1. Common
  2. Rare
  3. Epic
  4. Legendary
  5. Mythical
Best Horses: Mythical and Legendary horses have the best stats for speed, stamina, and mobility.

Can I replace my horse?

Yes, at Horse Stables

Visit any Horse Stables NPC to replace your current horse with a new one.

⚒️ Blacksmith & Repairs

Where is the Blacksmith?

Flint | Blacksmith in Main Town

Look for the Blacksmith NPC named Flint in the main town area.

What does the Blacksmith do?

The Blacksmith offers two services:

  • Can Questline: Special quest series
  • Mares Leg Repair: Fix rusted Mares Leg for 1500 Moola

I fished up a rusted Mares Leg. What do I do?

Take it to Flint (Blacksmith) to repair for 1500 Moola

The rusted Mares Leg is unusable until repaired. Once fixed, it becomes a functional secondary weapon.

Worth It: Mares Leg is a good secondary weapon (7 bullets, faster Winchester). 1500 Moola repair cost is reasonable.

📍 Spawn Points & Utility

How do I change my spawn point?

Find Spawn Point NPCs and pay 150 Moola

Steps:

  1. Find a Spawn Point NPC (various locations)
  2. Interact with the NPC
  3. Pay 150 Moola
  4. Your spawn point is now set to this location

Where should I set my spawn point?

Strategic locations to consider:

  • Near Mud Witch: Easy access to Card rolls and Dogbane trades
  • Near fishing spots: For Arrow Shard farming
  • Near KOTH areas: Quick response to Corpse Part events
  • Near Main Town: Central location for shops and services
Strategy: Change spawn points based on your current goals (fishing, KOTH, card farming, etc.).

What is Red Corner Fight Club?

PvP fist fighting arena in Main Town

Details:

  • Fist fight with other players
  • No weapons allowed
  • Cost if you lose: 300 Moola
Risk: Only fight if you're confident or have 300 Moola to spare. Losing is expensive.

🏴‍☠️ Outlaw Camp

Where is the Outlaw Camp Dealer?

Outlaw Camp location

The Outlaw Camp Dealer is located at the Outlaw Camp. May require Outlaw faction status to access.

What does the Outlaw Camp Dealer sell?

Extra guns and throwables

Available items:

  • Additional weapon options
  • Throwables (Dynamite, Molotov, Smoke Bombs)
  • Combat utilities

Do I need to be an Outlaw to use the Dealer?

Possibly yes

The Outlaw Camp Dealer may require Outlaw faction status to access. Choose your faction carefully if you want access to this vendor.
