Quick Facts

  • 📍 Location: Main Town (Red Corner area)
  • 🥊 Type: PvP fist fighting arena
  • 💸 Cost to lose: 300 Moola
  • 🚫 Weapons: Disabled inside the arena
  • 🏟️ Arena: Underground, teleported in after both players register

📋 How to Enter

  1. Approach the Fight Club NPC at the Red Corner in Main Town.
  2. Interact with the NPC to register for a fight.
  3. After interacting, two mats will appear in front of the NPC:
    • 🟢 Neon green — mat is vacant, step on it to register
    • 🔴 Neon red — mat is occupied by another player
  4. Step on a mat to register yourself as a fighter.
  5. Once both mats are occupied, both players are teleported to the underground arena below.
  6. Fight until one player reaches 0 HP.

⚔️ Fight Rules & Mechanics

✅ Allowed

  • 👊 Fist fighting
  • 🌀 Stand abilities
  • 🏃 Movement and dodging

🚫 Disabled

  • 🔫 Guns
  • 🧰 Utilities
  • 💣 Throwables
  • 🗡️ Other weapons

⚠️ The Spectator Loophole

Guns, utilities, and other items are disabled for the fighters inside the arena. However, spectators outside can throw items into the arena to interfere with the fight. This is an unofficial mechanic that can spice things up — or ruin a clean match depending on who you ask.

💸 Loss Penalty: If you lose the fight, you pay 300 Moola. Only enter if you're confident in your fist-fighting skills or have the Moola to spare.

💡 Tips

  • 🟢 Green mat = open — step on it immediately if you want to fight next.
  • 🔴 Red mat = taken — someone is already registered; wait or step on the other mat to challenge them.
  • 💰 Check your Moola before entering — losing costs 300 Moola, so make sure you can afford it.
  • 👀 Watch out for spectators — people outside can throw items in. Position yourself away from the edges if possible.
  • 🌀 Stands still work — if you have a Stand, you can use its abilities inside the arena.

Bridger Western Red Corner Fight Club Wiki – Complete PvP Arena Guide 2026

The Bridger Western Red Corner Fight Club is the game's dedicated PvP fist fighting arena, located in the Main Town area near the Red Corner spawn point. To enter, players interact with the Fight Club NPC, which activates two glowing mats on the ground. A neon green mat indicates the spot is vacant and available to register, while a neon red mat means another player has already claimed that position. Once both mats are occupied, both fighters are automatically teleported to the underground arena below to begin their match.

Inside the Bridger Western fight arena, guns, utilities, throwables, and other weapons are completely disabled — the fight is purely fist-based, with Stand abilities still available to those who have them. The match ends when one player reaches 0 HP. The key risk of the Bridger Western fight club is the 300 Moola loss penalty: the losing player automatically pays 300 Moola, so it's important to only enter if you're confident or have enough currency to absorb the loss. One unofficial mechanic to be aware of is that spectators standing outside the arena can throw items in, which can unexpectedly affect the outcome of a fight.
