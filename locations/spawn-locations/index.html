How Spawn Points Work

📍 Changing Your Spawn Point

Interact with the corresponding NPC at any spawn location and pay 150 Moola to set it as your new spawn point. Spawn locations are marked as small dots on the world map.

Note: Outlaws cannot set spawn at Inlaw spawns, and Inlaws cannot set spawn at Outlaw spawns. Each faction has its own set of spawn locations.

🛡️ Anti-Spawn-Kill Protection

Dying at your own spawn point has a 30% chance to automatically change your spawn to a different location. This prevents spawn killing and stops enemies from camping your respawn point.

This mechanic also prevents you from immediately returning to the same spot after being killed there.

⚖️ Inlaw Spawn Locations

Available to Inlaw (law-abiding) faction players only

🏘️ Ridge B. County

The main town spawn. When you respawn here, you appear in one of three buildings at random:

  • 🏨 Hotel — Main town hotel building
  • 🏥 LP. Haris Medical Help — Medical building
  • 🏚️ Random Red Building — One of the red buildings in town

This is the most central Inlaw spawn, close to shops and NPCs.

⛏️ Digsite

Spawns you at the caravan adjacent to the digsite. This is a more isolated spawn away from the main town, useful for players who want to avoid the busy town center.

Good option if you want to avoid Outlaw ambushes near the main town.

🔫 Outlaw Spawn Locations

Available to Outlaw faction players only — 5 spawn options

🏕️ Campsite

Spawns you just outside of the swamp area. Convenient for players who frequently visit the Mud Witch's Hut or operate in the swamp region.

Close to the Mud Witch — useful for card rolls and Dogbane trades.

🏰 Outlaw Hideout

Spawns you inside the woods fortress. A well-protected spawn point surrounded by the fortress walls, making it harder for Inlaws to ambush you immediately on spawn.

One of the safer Outlaw spawns due to the fortress structure.

⛰️ Outlaw Hills

Spawns you at one of 2 campfires in the hills area. The random selection between two campfires adds unpredictability, making it harder for enemies to camp your exact spawn.

Two campfire options reduce spawn camping risk.

🥊 Red Corner

Spawns you at the Red Corner location in the tunnel. The Red Corner is also home to the Red Corner Fight Club where players can fist fight (losing costs 300 Moola).

Tunnel location provides some natural cover on spawn.

🏜️ Sandtown

Spawns you in a random building at Sandtown. Similar to Ridge B. County for Inlaws, the random building selection helps prevent spawn camping.

Desert area spawn — good for Outlaws operating in the eastern region.

💡 Spawn Strategy Tips

Choosing the Right Spawn

  • 🏪 Near shops → Ridge B. County (Inlaw) for quick access to Gun Store, General Store, Banker
  • 🧙 Near Mud Witch → Campsite (Outlaw) for card rolls and Dogbane trades
  • 🛡️ Safe spawn → Outlaw Hideout for fortress protection, Digsite for isolation
  • 🎯 Anti-camp → Outlaw Hills (2 campfires) or Ridge B. County (3 buildings) for unpredictable spawns

Spawn Point Rules

  • 💰 Changing spawn costs 150 Moola — interact with the location NPC
  • 🔒 Faction locked — Outlaws cannot use Inlaw spawns and vice versa
  • 🎲 30% auto-change — dying at your own spawn may randomly move it to prevent spawn killing
  • 🗺️ Spawn dots are marked on the world map — accurate to the exact location

Bridger Western Spawn Locations Wiki & Trello – Complete 2026 Spawn Guide

Bridger Western spawn locations are divided between two factions: Inlaw (law-abiding players) and Outlaw (bandit players). Inlaw players can choose between Ridge B. County — which spawns you in one of three buildings including the Hotel, LP. Haris Medical Help, or a Random Red Building — and the Digsite, which spawns you at the adjacent caravan. Outlaw players have five spawn options: Campsite (outside the swamp), Outlaw Hideout (woods fortress), Outlaw Hills (one of two campfires), Red Corner (tunnel location), and Sandtown (random building). All spawn points are marked as small dots on the official Bridger Western world map.

To change your Bridger Western spawn point, interact with the NPC at your desired spawn location and pay 150 Moola. This is a one-time cost per spawn change. The game includes an anti-spawn-kill mechanic: dying at your own spawn point has a 30% chance to automatically move your spawn to a different location, preventing enemies from camping your respawn. This system also stops you from immediately returning to the same spot after being killed there. Faction restrictions apply — Outlaws cannot set spawn at Inlaw spawn points and vice versa, keeping the two factions' territories separate.

Choosing the right Bridger Western spawn location depends on your playstyle. Inlaw players who want quick access to shops should use Ridge B. County, which is close to the Gun Store, General Store, and Banker. Outlaw players who frequently visit the Mud Witch for card rolls should use the Campsite spawn near the swamp. For maximum spawn safety, the Outlaw Hideout's fortress walls provide natural protection, while spawns with multiple random locations (Ridge B. County's 3 buildings, Outlaw Hills' 2 campfires) make it harder for enemies to predict your exact spawn position.
