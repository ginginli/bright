Key Locations on the Map

🏘️ Main Town

  • Gun Store: Sells most guns in the game
  • General Store: Sells knives, cowboy hat, poncho, lasso, coin, lantern
  • Banker: Store guns, utilities, items (NOT kept upon wipe)
  • Red Corner Fight Club: Fist fight with other players (losing costs 300 Moola)

✂️ Customization Services

  • Tailor: Reroll Shirt/Pants (15 Moola), Accessories (30 Moola)
  • Barbershop: Reroll hairstyle (25 Moola), hair color (15 Moola)

🗺️ Special Locations

  • Mud Witch's Hut (Swamp): Read fortune for Cards (150 Moola), trade Dogbane Herb (2500 Moola / age -20 years / wipe Stand)
  • Outlaw Camp Dealer: Extra guns, throwables, etc.
  • Horse Stables: Replace horse, press M to see stats (Speed, Stamina, Jump, Damage, Scale, Turn). Mythical and Legendary horses exist
  • Flint | Blacksmith: Can Questline, fix rusted Mares Leg (1500 Moola)

📍 Utility NPCs

  • Spawn Point NPCs: Change spawn point for 150 Moola (various locations)

About the Bridger Western Map

The Bridger Western map shows all key locations across Ridge B Valley. The Main Town contains essential services: Gun Store (sells most guns), General Store (knives, cowboy hat, poncho, lasso, coin, lantern), Banker (item storage, NOT kept upon wipe), Tailor (reroll shirt/pants 15 Moola, accessories 30 Moola), Barbershop (hairstyle 25 Moola, hair color 15 Moola), and Red Corner Fight Club (fist fights, losing costs 300 Moola).

Special locations include the Mud Witch's Hut in the Swamp area (Card rolls 150 Moola, Dogbane Herb trades for 2500 Moola/age regression -20 years/Stand removal), Blacksmith Flint (Can Questline, Mares Leg repair 1500 Moola), Horse Stables (replace horse, press M for stats: Speed, Stamina, Jump, Damage, Scale, Turn - Mythical and Legendary horses available), and Outlaw Camp Dealer (extra guns and throwables).

Spawn Point NPCs are located at various positions across the map, allowing players to change their respawn location for 150 Moola. This provides strategic positioning for accessing different areas like the Swamp, fishing spots, or KOTH zones. Use this Bridger Western map to plan efficient routes between locations and optimize your gameplay in Ridge B Valley.
