Complete Guide to Shops & Services

Find all NPCs, shops, and services in Ridge B Valley. Gun Store, Mud Witch, Horse Stables, Banker, and more.

🏘️ Main Town

🔫 Gun Store

Location: Main Town

Services: Sells most guns in the game

Available Weapons:

  • • Primary weapons (revolvers, rifles, bow)
  • • Secondary weapons (requires Tier 3)
  • • Ammo and ammunition supplies

🏪 General Store

Location: Main Town

Services: Sells utilities and equipment

Available Items:

  • • Knives
  • • Cowboy Hat (headshot protection)
  • • Poncho (damage reduction)
  • • Lasso
  • • Coin (ricochet item)
  • • Lantern

🏦 Banker

Location: Main Town

Services: Item storage system

Storage Details:

  • • Store guns, utilities, and items
  • • Access stored items anytime
  • • Safe from death/loss
Important: All items in bank are NOT kept upon server wipe. Bank is temporary storage only.

🥊 Red Corner Fight Club

Location: Main Town

Services: PvP fist fighting arena

Details:

  • • Fist fight with other players
  • • Cost: 300 Moola if you lose
  • • No weapons allowed
Risk: Losing costs 300 Moola. Only fight if you're confident or have Moola to spare.

✨ Customization Services

👔 Tailor

Location: Main Town

Services: Clothing customization

Prices:

  • • Reroll Shirt & Pants: 15 Moola
  • • Reroll Accessory: 30 Moola
Cosmetic Only: Clothing changes are purely visual and don't affect gameplay.

✂️ Barbershop

Location: Main Town

Services: Hair customization

Prices:

  • • Reroll Hairstyle: 25 Moola
  • • Reroll Hair Color: 15 Moola

🌟 Special Locations

🧙 Mud Witch's Hut

Location: Swamp area

Services: Fortune reading and Dogbane Herb trades

Services:

1. Read Your Fortune (Cards):

  • • Cost: 150 Moola per roll
  • • Choose 1 card from 3 random options
  • • Maximum 3 cards per character

2. Offer Dogbane Herb:

  • • Option 1: Get 2500 Moola
  • • Option 2: Decrease age by 20 years
  • • Option 3: Wipe/remove your Stand
Dogbane Herb: Rare 4% drop from chests. Very valuable for age management or Stand rerolling.

🏴‍☠️ Outlaw Camp Dealer

Location: Outlaw Camp

Services: Sells extra guns and throwables

Available Items:

  • • Additional weapon options
  • • Throwables (Dynamite, Molotov, etc.)
  • • Combat utilities
Outlaw Only: May require Outlaw faction status to access.

🐴 Horse Stables | Horse Info

Location: Various locations

Services: Horse replacement and information

Features:

  • • Replace your current horse
  • • View horse stats (press M)
  • • Stats: Speed, Stamina, Jump, Damage, Scale, Turn
  • • Horse rarities: Common, Rare, Epic, Legendary, Mythical
Horse Stats: Press M to see your horse's stats. Mythical and Legendary horses have the best stats.

⚒️ Flint | Blacksmith

Location: Main Town

Services: Weapon repairs and quests

Services:

  • • Can Questline: Special quest series
  • • Mares Leg Repair: Fix rusted Mares Leg for 1500 Moola
Mares Leg: If you fish up a rusted Mares Leg, bring it here to repair for 1500 Moola.

🎯 Utility NPCs

📍 Spawn Point NPCs

Location: Various locations across the map

Service: Change your spawn point

Details:

  • • Cost: 150 Moola
  • • Interact with NPC to set spawn point
  • • Respawn at this location after death
  • • Multiple spawn points available across map
