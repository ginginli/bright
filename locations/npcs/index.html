⚒️ Flint | Blacksmith

📍 Location

Found in front of Ridge B. County, specifically in front of the bridge near the train stop.

🔧 Services

  • • Gives you the Can Questline
  • • Repairs a rusted Mares Leg for 1,500 Moola

🎯 Can Questline

Shoot a can thrown by Flint the required number of times before it lands. The number of hits required is indicated in the dialogue.

Quest Stages

Stage 1 — Hit the can 3 times
Stage 2 — Hit the can 4 times
Stage 3 — Hit the can 6 times

Rewards & Penalties

  • ✅ Success: +100 Moola, +30 EXP per completion
  • ❌ Failure: -100 Moola
  • ⏱️ Cooldown: 75–95 seconds between attempts (excluding the last)
Tip: This quest is very difficult without Ocular Prowess. It is recommended to reach at least Tier 2 or 3 before attempting, or use the Evil Eye card. Try not to be blinded by the sun.

🌀 Gyro

📍 Location & Overview

Allows you to buy Spin for $25,000 Moola after fulfilling his requirements.

  • • Can be found in various points around the map, particularly behind trees in the forest or swamp
  • • Will not give you the questline if you are above age 40

🎯 Requirements to Unlock Spin

1️⃣ First Requirement: Resonate with Spin activity found around the map

2️⃣ Second Requirement: Speak with Gyro to initiate questline

3️⃣ Third Requirement: "Toughen your knuckles"

Obtain a 15–20 killstreak / consecutive wins in Fight Club, then reset and speak to Gyro again.

4️⃣ Fourth Requirement: Obtain the "Duelist's Mark"

Defeat the Duelist NPC in a rare encounter.

Final Step: When all requirements are done, speak to Gyro again and pay him $25,000 Moola to obtain Spin.

🌀 After Obtaining Spin

You are able to advance your spin mastery by charging your spin bar repeatedly, causing enhanced throws to consume less bar.

At max proficiency you are able to purchase his grills for $2,000 Moola.

Bridger Western NPCs are key characters scattered across Ridge B Valley who provide quests, repairs, and services. Flint the Blacksmith is located in front of Ridge B. County, specifically in front of the bridge near the train stop. He is the same NPC who gives you the Can Questline and can repair a rusted Mares Leg for 1,500 Moola.

Players searching for Bridger Western Flint Blacksmith location or how to repair Mares Leg in Bridger Western will find Flint at the bridge near the train stop in Ridge B. County. The Bridger Western Can Questline is also started by talking to Flint. Bringing a rusted Mares Leg — obtained from fishing at a 2% drop rate — to Flint costs 1,500 Moola to fix.
