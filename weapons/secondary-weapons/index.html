Bridger Western Secondary Weapons – Complete Tier 3 Guide 2026

Looking for Bridger Western secondary weapons? Remember that you cannot equip both primary and secondary weapons until reaching Bridger Western tier 3. This guide covers all 6 secondary weapons available for dual wielding. The Bridger Western Mare's Leg holds 7 bullets and functions as a lower damage, faster version of the Winchester Repeater, perfect for mobile combat. The Bridger Western Sabre is an awesome melee weapon that can parry other sabres and even vampires, making it essential for close-quarters combat.

The Bridger Western Colt Ocelot is a higher damage revolver with unique ricochet mechanics – bullets bounce 3 times, allowing for creative tactical plays and hitting enemies behind cover. For devastating close-range damage, the Bridger Western DB Shotgun (double barrel shotgun) shoots twice with very wide spread, perfect for crowd control. The Bridger Western Tommy Gun holds 25 bullets and shoots with heavy spread and speed, making it ideal for suppressive fire and sustained combat engagements.

The Bridger Western Maverick 88 is a low spread pump action shotgun that shoots 6 pellets, offering more precision than the DB Shotgun while maintaining excellent close-range damage. Understanding Bridger Western dual wield mechanics and Bridger Western tier 3 requirements is crucial for maximizing your combat effectiveness. Players often search for Bridger Western best secondary weapon combinations with primary weapons. Remember that Bridger Western shotguns excel at close range, while the Bridger Western Tommy Gun provides sustained fire for medium-range engagements.
