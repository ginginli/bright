Bridger Western Primary Weapons – Complete 2026 Alpha Database

Searching for Bridger Western primary weapons? This complete guide covers all 9 primary weapons available in Ridge B Valley. The Bridger Western Taurus .357 is your trusty starting pistol with mid-range stats across the board, making it reliable for beginners. The Bridger Western Schofield 6 offers average performance with fairly quick reload speed, while the Bridger Western Whitney Dragoon provides high damage at the cost of slow reloading. For players seeking high fire rate, the Bridger Western Dual Derringers deliver low damage but exceptional speed and fast reloading.

The Bridger Western Mauser is a low recoil, low damage pistol with fairly high fire rate, perfect for sustained combat. The Bridger Western Winchester Repeater is essentially an 8-bullet revolver with more range and high fire rate, making it one of the most versatile primary weapons. For ranged combat, the Bridger Western Bow functions as a mid-range sniper with lower damage and slower shooting speed, but offers unique arrow switching mechanics – right click to switch between Silver Arrows and Dynamite Arrows for tactical advantages.

The Bridger Western Malcom .70 is a 4-bullet scoped rifle with long range capabilities, dealing 50 damage on headshots, making it the premier sniper choice. The Bridger Western Bolt Action Rifle is currently unobtainable and will be remodeled in future updates. Understanding Bridger Western gun stats and Bridger Western weapon damage is crucial for optimal loadout selection. Remember that Bridger Western revolvers have wasteful reload mechanics, and having a Stand will decrease your base gun damages across all primary weapons.
