Core Weapon Mechanics

🎯 Tier 3 Dual Wield System

You cannot have both a primary and secondary weapon until your character reaches tier 3.

This is a core progression mechanic in Bridger Western. Players must choose between equipping a primary weapon OR a secondary weapon until they reach tier 3. Once tier 3 is achieved, you can equip both simultaneously for maximum combat effectiveness.

🌟 Stand Penalty

Having a Stand will decrease your base gun damages.

Players who obtain a Stand will experience reduced weapon damage. This creates a strategic trade-off between Stand abilities and gun combat effectiveness. Consider your playstyle when deciding whether to pursue Stand acquisition.

🔄 Revolver Reload Mechanics

Revolvers have wasteful reloads.

Revolvers in Bridger Western feature less efficient reload mechanics compared to other weapon types. This affects weapons like the Taurus .357, Schofield 6, Whitney Dragoon, and Colt Ocelot. Plan your engagements accordingly and consider reload timing in combat situations.
